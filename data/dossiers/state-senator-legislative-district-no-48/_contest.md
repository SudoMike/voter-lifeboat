## What this office does

One of two chambers of the Washington State Legislature. State senators serve
4-year terms, vote on the state operating/capital/transportation budgets, state
taxes, and all state law (housing, education funding, public safety, health
care, climate policy).

## Race dynamics

- Depth is light: this race has a single candidate (incumbent Sen. Vandana
  Slatter, D), who advances from the primary to the general election
  regardless of the primary result.
- LD 48 covers Redmond, Bellevue, Kirkland, and the Points communities and is
  heavily Democratic.
- Slatter was appointed to this seat in January 2025 to replace Patty Kuderer
  (elected Insurance Commissioner) and retained it in a November 2025 special
  election in which she was challenged by Rep. Amy Walen.
