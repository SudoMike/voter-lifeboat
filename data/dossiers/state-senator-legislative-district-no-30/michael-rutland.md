## Background
Republican-preference first-time candidate from Federal Way [S1][S2]. Retired U.S. Air Force Senior Master Sergeant with 24 years of service, including Desert Shield and Desert Storm; former Boeing leader with four decades of management experience [S1][S2]. BS in management (magna cum laude), University of Phoenix [S1]. 41-year member of the Knights of Columbus; volunteer with Coats for Kids and Washington Special Olympics in Federal Way [S1][S2].

## Positions
- Affordability/taxes: opposes new income taxes; wants to repeal policies he says drive up fuel and grocery prices; calls for "responsible budgeting instead of higher taxes" [S1][S2].
- Education: supports high academic standards, safe schools, career and technical education, and parental involvement; says schools should not advance "political agendas" [S1][S2].
- Public safety: supports law enforcement paired with accountability measures [S1][S2].
- Local control: wants local leaders to have decision-making flexibility [S2].
- Frames candidacy as bringing "balance" to Olympia [S1].
