## Background
Cliff Moon is a Republican candidate who has lived in the 1st Legislative District for over a decade [S2]. His only elected experience is as a Precinct Committee Officer in the district [S1]. He reports sixty-three years of experience in the construction trades and fifty-four years in small business ownership [S1]. He holds a B.S. in Physical Oceanography and an M.S. in Civil Engineering, both from the University of Washington, and has taught entrepreneurship and business courses as a visiting lecturer at universities in Kazakhstan, India, and UW-Bothell [S1].

## Positions
- **Taxes / cost of living:** Says layers of taxes are driving up the cost of gas, groceries, and utilities; advocates property-tax relief so families are not "taxed out of the neighborhoods they built" [S1][S2].
- **Public safety:** Supports law enforcement, full funding for local police, and criminal-justice accountability [S1][S2].
- **Education:** Says schools should focus on academics and parents should raise their children [S1].
- **Immigration:** Supports legal immigration [S1].
- **Small business / regulation:** Promises to cut red tape and reduce regulations on businesses [S2].
- **Infrastructure:** Prioritizes investment in roads, bridges, and transit systems [S2].
