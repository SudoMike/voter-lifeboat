## Background
Elle Nguyen is a Republican candidate from Mercer Island [S3]. She describes herself as a widowed mother, small business owner, law school graduate, and community advocate [S2]. Her pamphlet lists a B.A. in theology/religious studies, a Master of Divinity, and a Juris Doctor [S1]; Ballotpedia reports degrees from Regent University (2010, 2012) and a J.D. from Seattle University (2016), and career experience as a real estate investor [S4]. She has not held elected office: she ran for the Mercer Island School Board in 2021 (advancing past the primary) and for LD 41 Position 2 in 2022, losing in the primary [S1][S4]. She chairs Connect Care Alliance, involving community health, youth programs, and public accountability initiatives, and does homeless-outreach and family-support volunteer work [S1].

## Positions
- **Fiscal policy:** priorities are "stopping wasteful spending, balancing the budget, and protecting taxpayers from unnecessary tax increases" [S2].
- **Public safety:** supports law enforcement and neighborhood safety [S1][S2].
- **Transparency:** calls for open government, honest accounting, and accountability [S1][S2].
- **Family / social policy:** emphasizes protecting children, strengthening families, parental rights, "traditional values," and youth well-being [S1][S2].
- **Cost of living / small business:** cites economic opportunity for working families and support for small businesses [S1][S2].
- **Constitutional rights:** lists defending constitutional protections as a core plank [S2].

## Record
- No elected office held; candidacies in 2021 (school board) and 2022 (LD 41 Position 2) [S1][S4].
- Campaign finance: no contributions for a 2026 Elle Nguyen committee appear in the PDC contributions dataset as of 2026-07-16 [S5].

## Endorsements
- Her website cites testimonials from community figures identified by role (Mercer Island resident, local business owner, Bellevue Chamber member, civic leaders) rather than named organizational endorsements [S2]. No organizational endorsements found.
