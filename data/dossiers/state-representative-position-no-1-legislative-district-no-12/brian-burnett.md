## Background
Incumbent Republican state representative for the 12th Legislative District (all of Chelan County plus parts of Snohomish and King counties), first elected in 2024 and serving his first term [S1][S2]. Elected Chelan County Sheriff for three consecutive terms (2011–2022) after more than 25 years in law enforcement [S1][S2]. Past president of the Washington Association of Sheriffs & Police Chiefs and of the Washington State Sheriffs Association; served as a governor-appointed commissioner on the Washington State Criminal Justice Training Commission [S1][S2]. Currently Director of Law Enforcement Services for Seattle's Finest Security [S1][S2]. FBI National Academy graduate (Class 262) [S1]. Lives in Wenatchee with his wife April; community roles include camp director for Royal Family Kids (foster children's summer camp) and boards of NCW Chaplains Foundation and UVCares [S1][S2].

## Positions
- Public safety: reducing crime and "victim-centered justice that holds criminals accountable" [S1].
- Parental rights: protecting "your role as a parent" [S1].
- Fiscal: prioritizing taxpayer dollars; reducing burdens on small businesses [S1].
- Local control: returning decision-making to local communities [S1].

## Record
- First-term incumbent; committee assignments described as aligned with his law-enforcement background [S2]. No specific bills cited in light-depth sources.
