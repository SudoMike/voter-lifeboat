## What the office does
One of two state representatives for the 12th Legislative District in the Washington State House. After the 2024 court-ordered redistricting, the district covers all of Chelan County plus parts of Snohomish and King counties, so only a portion of its voters are in King County. Two-year term.

## Race dynamics
Two candidates — first-term incumbent Republican Brian Burnett (former three-term Chelan County Sheriff) and Democrat Stacy Willoughby (Chelan bank branch manager and nonprofit board president) — so both advance from the primary to the November general election regardless of primary results. Research depth is intentionally light for this reason.

## Differentiating axes
- Career contrast: 25+ years law enforcement/elected sheriff (Burnett) vs. 25 years financial services and anti-poverty nonprofit leadership (Willoughby).
- Issue lead: public safety, parental rights, and local control (Burnett) vs. affordability, rural tax fairness, housing, and trades training (Willoughby).
- Endorsements: Willoughby lists Democratic party organizations and labor; Burnett's pamphlet lists none.
