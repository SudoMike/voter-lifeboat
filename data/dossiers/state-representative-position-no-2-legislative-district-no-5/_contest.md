## What the office does
One of two state representatives for the 5th Legislative District (Issaquah, Snoqualmie, North Bend, Maple Valley, Black Diamond, Enumclaw, parts of Sammamish) in the Washington State House. Two-year term.

## Race dynamics
Two candidates — incumbent Democrat Lisa Callan and Republican challenger Patrick Peacock — so both advance from the primary to the November general election regardless of primary results. Research depth is intentionally light for this reason.

## Differentiating axes
- Incumbent with budget-committee leadership and an education/childcare record vs. a first-time candidate with a military-intelligence and security career.
- Taxes: Callan wants a tax code that "doesn't squeeze working people"; Peacock opposes any income tax and wants fuel-tax rollbacks.
- Housing: both name affordability, but Callan emphasizes workforce-housing investment while Peacock emphasizes deregulation of construction.
- Endorsement bases: labor/education/environmental/reproductive-rights coalition (Callan) vs. Republican party organizations (Peacock).
