## Background

Dimitri Georgakopoulos (legal name Theodoros Dimitrios Georgakopoulos per PDC filings [S3]) was raised in Montlake, attended Seattle public schools, graduated from Garfield High School, and earned a degree at Seattle University [S1]. He describes nearly three decades as a Seattle entrepreneur: first owning Greek restaurants across the city, then working for the last 13 years as a licensed real estate broker [S1], currently with Windermere [S2]. He submitted no education or occupation information to the voters' pamphlet [S1]. He describes earlier experience in youth coaching, day camps, after-school program management, and business consulting [S2]. He has not held elected office and describes himself as "not a career politician" [S1].

## Positions

### Public safety and homelessness
- Says Aurora "continues to be plagued by sex trafficking and predatory businesses" [S1].
- Says he will "work with the city council members, the mayor and SPD, to keep our streets free of Drug abusers, homeless encampments and violent offenders" [S2].

### Housing
- Criticizes low-income housing being "concentrated in District 5 without equity or a real plan" [S1]. No affirmative housing platform was found beyond this critique [S1][S2].

### Business and economy
- Focus on "real support for District 5 businesses"; says entrepreneurs are "left without a lifeline" and wants to "reshape the policies to create a more prosperous vision for the business community" [S1][S2].
- Criticizes bike lanes multiplying "while our commercial corridors go quiet" [S1].

### Approach
- Emphasizes "accountability from our institutions" and "equitable solutions — developed in partnership with community leaders and the private sector" [S1]; says he seeks solutions that are "scalable, actionable" [S2].
- Expresses skepticism of familiar campaign promises including "Tax the rich" [S1].

## Endorsements

None found on his campaign website or in coverage reviewed [S2].

## Campaign finance

- PDC records show $2,050 in reported contributions for the 2026 cycle as of 2026-07-16, far below the other three candidates [S3].
- Participates in the Democracy Voucher Program at "Signed Pledge" status (collecting qualifying contributions and signatures; not yet qualified to redeem vouchers) [S4].
