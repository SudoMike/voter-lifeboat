## Background

John P Gibbons is running with a Republican Party preference for State Representative, Position 2, in the 45th Legislative District [S1]. He submitted no candidate statement, no elected experience, no professional experience, no education, and no community service information to the voters' pamphlet, and lists no campaign website [S1]. No contributions for a 2026 Gibbons legislative campaign appear in PDC data as of the research date [S2].

A Republican candidate named John Gibbons previously ran twice for the State House in this same district: in 2022 he lost the general election for LD45 Position 2 to incumbent Larry Springer 27.1% to 72.7%, and in 2020 he lost the general election for LD45 Position 1 to incumbent Roger Goodman 28.3% to 71.6% [S3]. Given the matching name, district, and party preference, this is likely the same person, but identity could not be conclusively verified because the candidate provided no biographical information [S1][S3]. (Note: the Ballotpedia page for this name also includes a 2024 Democratic primary run in House District 23 in Kitsap County; that candidacy is not attributed here because the party and jurisdiction differ and the merge could not be verified.)
