## Background

Attorney (Washington State Bar), investor, and Washington-licensed
nutritionist; no prior elected experience [S1]. Fourth-generation
Washingtonian, born and raised on Mercer Island [S1][S3]. BBA magna cum
laude, George Washington University; JD, University of Miami; MS in
Nutrition, National University of Natural Medicine [S1]. Volunteer with
Democracy Postcarders, Mercer Island Rotary Club, and area chambers of
commerce [S1]. Prefers the Independent Party [S1][S3].

Note: his campaign website contained no substantive policy content when
fetched, so this dossier rests essentially on the pamphlet statement.

## Positions

- **Fiscal policy:** Argues state spending "more than doubled in two
  decades" while accountability, affordability, public safety, and health
  outcomes worsened; calls for fiscal discipline and treating tax dollars
  accountably [S1].
- **Affordability:** Prioritizes affordability for Washington families
  [S1].
- **Health:** Improving health outcomes, drawing on his nutrition
  background [S1].
- **Approach:** Describes himself as thinking independently, following
  evidence, and valuing "outcomes over ideology" [S1].

## Scoring notes

- The fiscal-restraint candidate in this race: critique of Olympia spending
