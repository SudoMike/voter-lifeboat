## Background

Incumbent State Representative for LD 41 Position 2 [S1][S3]. House Deputy
Majority Leader; member of the Appropriations, Civil Rights & Judiciary,
Community Safety, Education, Finance, Healthcare & Wellness, Housing, and
Rules committees [S1]. Pharmacist by profession; former Bellevue School
District Board President and former Vice-President of the Washington State
School Directors' Association [S1]. Describes herself as the first refugee
elected to the Washington Legislature [S1]. University of Washington School
of Pharmacy graduate [S1].

## Positions

- **Healthcare:** Protecting access to affordable, high-quality care and
  expanding behavioral health services are stated top priorities [S1].
- **Climate:** Supports "meaningful action on climate change" and
  scientific innovation [S1].
- **Education:** Educational opportunity and workforce development focus,
  drawing on school-board background [S1][S2].
- **Community/civil rights:** Emphasizes a welcoming, diverse community and
  protecting rights of all people; advancing a "just, inclusive, and
  equitable society" [S1][S2].
- **Transportation:** Improving transportation across the region [S1].
