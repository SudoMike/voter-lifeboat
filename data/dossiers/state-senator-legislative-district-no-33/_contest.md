## What this office does
A Washington State Senator is the district's single member in the 49-seat Senate, elected to a four-year term (this race fills the seat following a 2024 appointment). Senators write and vote on state law and budgets and shape tax, housing, healthcare, education, and transportation policy.

## The race
Depth: light. LD 33 covers South King County communities around Sea-Tac Airport (SeaTac, Des Moines, Burien, Normandy Park, parts of Kent) [S1]. **Only one candidate filed — Tina L. Orwall (D) — so she advances from the August 4, 2026 top-two primary to the November general election unopposed on the primary ballot** [S1].

- **Tina L. Orwall (D, incumbent)** — appointed to the Senate in December 2024 to succeed longtime Senator Karen Keiser after 15+ years as a 33rd District state representative; career social worker with a behavioral-health background [S1][S2].

## Differentiating issue axes
Not applicable — this is a single-candidate contest, so no candidate-vs-candidate axes exist. The dossier documents her record and priorities (housing, behavioral health, veterans, AI consumer protections, airport pollution mitigation) for voter information rather than comparison [S1].
