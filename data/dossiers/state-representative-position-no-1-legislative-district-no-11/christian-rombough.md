## Background

- U.S. Army veteran (19D cavalry scout) and journeyman ironworker out of Ironworkers Local 86; describes his work as helping build out Washington's infrastructure [S1].
- Self-describes as a native Washingtonian and father; no elected experience listed [S1].
- Community service listed: providing end-of-life care for family members during cancer treatment [S1].
- His campaign website listed in the pamphlet contained no content as of 2026-07-16 ("under construction") [S2].

## Positions

- Argues "Olympia has grown too wasteful, too arrogant, and too disconnected from working families"; pledges to cut red tape and demand accountability [S1].
- Supports good-paying jobs and says he will "defend trade unions" — an unusual pairing with his Republican party preference [S1].
- Supports restoring local control and backing public safety [S1].
- Signed the U.S. Term Limits pledge (announced July 2, 2026) committing, if elected, to support an Article V convention resolution for congressional term limits [S3].
