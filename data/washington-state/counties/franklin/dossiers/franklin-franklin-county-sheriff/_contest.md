## What this office does

This office administers the statutory responsibilities of Franklin County Sheriff, including public oversight, budgeting, and services within its jurisdiction [S1].

## Race dynamics

Three law-enforcement professionals seek the open sheriff position: Kohl St. Peter, Undersheriff Monty Huber, and Connell Police Chief Chris Lee. [S1].
