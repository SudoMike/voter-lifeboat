## What it does

Lifts the county's regular property-tax levy lid for taxes collected in 2027, under Resolution 48-2026 [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: The measure sets a rate of $1.15 per $1,000 assessed value, equal to $115 per $100,000 or $575 on $500,000 [S1].

## Arguments for

Approval supplies the county with the additional regular-levy revenue authorized by the resolution; no official pro statement was available in the reviewed material [S1].

## Arguments against

Rejection avoids the proposed levy-lid increase; no official con statement was available in the reviewed material [S1].

## Axis notes

- Higher county property-tax revenue versus limiting the regular levy [S1].
