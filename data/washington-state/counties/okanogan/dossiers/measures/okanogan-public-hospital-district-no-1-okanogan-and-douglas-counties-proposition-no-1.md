## What it does

Renews a one-year special property-tax levy for hospital-district operations [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: The levy raises $1,460,000 at approximately $0.33 per $1,000 assessed value, or about $165 on $500,000 [S1].

## Arguments for

Approval supplies the stated one-year operating revenue; no official pro statement was available in the reviewed material [S1].

## Arguments against

Rejection avoids the special property tax; no official con statement was available in the reviewed material [S1].
