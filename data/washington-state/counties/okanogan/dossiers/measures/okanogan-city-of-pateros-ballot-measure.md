## What it does

Continues the City of Pateros emergency-medical-services property-tax levy for six years [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: $0.50 per $1,000 assessed value, equal to $50 per $100,000 or $250 on $500,000 [S1].

## Arguments for

Approval continues the named EMS revenue for six years; no official pro statement was available in the reviewed material [S1].

## Arguments against

Rejection avoids the continued EMS property tax; no official con statement was available in the reviewed material [S1].

## Axis notes

- Continued municipal EMS funding versus property-tax restraint [S1].
