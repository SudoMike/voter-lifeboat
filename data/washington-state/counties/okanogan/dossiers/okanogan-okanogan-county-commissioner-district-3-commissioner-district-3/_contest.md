## What this office does

The three-member board adopts the county budget and policy and oversees county services, land use, and emergency-management priorities.

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Jon Neal faces Trinity Hightower-Stucker. Their published records emphasize county experience and regional boards for Neal, and budgeting, wildfire preparation, permitting, and rural economic issues for Hightower-Stucker.
