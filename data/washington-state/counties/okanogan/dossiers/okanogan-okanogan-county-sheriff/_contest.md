## What this office does

The sheriff leads county law enforcement, patrol and investigations, and operations associated with the county jail.

## Race dynamics

Appointed Sheriff Jodie Barcus faces deputy Gisberth “Giz” Gonzalez. Both stress law-enforcement experience; their published priorities differ in emphasis across agency management, investigations, patrol, transparency, and rehabilitation.
