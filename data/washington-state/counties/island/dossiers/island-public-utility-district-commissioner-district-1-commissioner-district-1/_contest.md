## What this office does

The three-member PUD commission establishes utility policies and rates, guides electric and water operations, and appoints the chief executive [S1].

## Race dynamics

The official Island County sample ballot lists incumbent Sid Logan, Bruce King, and Island County Commissioner Janet St Clair for District 1 [S1]. District 1 includes Camano Island as part of the Snohomish County PUD service area.
