August 4, 2026 Primary Ballot Resolutions

The following resolution was submitted to Mason County for a ballot measure that includes voters in Kitsap County in the April 28, 2026 Special Election.

Ballot ​Title

City of Bremerton
 ​​Proposition 1 

PUBLIC SAFETY AND GOVERNMENTAL SERVICES LEVY

The Bremerton City Council adopted Resolution 3409 concerning property taxes for public safety and governmental services. If approved, this proposition would fund City services, including firefighting equipment, staffing, and facilities. It would increase the City's regular property tax rate by $0.48 to a total authorized rate of $1.95 per $1,000 of assessed value for collection in 2027. The 2027 levy amount would be used to calculate subsequent levies. Should this proposition be approved?

​Kitsap County Fire Protection District No. 18 (Poulsbo Fire)


​Proposition 1
​BONDS FOR FIRE AND EMERGENCY RESPONSE FACILITIES, VEHICLES AND EQUIPMENT

The Board of Fire Commissioners of Kitsap County Fire Protection District No. 18 (Poulsbo Fire Department) adopted Resolution No. 26-05, concerning emergency response facilities to protect public health, life and property. This proposition would authorize the District to: acquire, construct and renovate firefighting and emergency response facilities, vehicles and equipment (including renovating the fleet facilities; acquiring firefighting apparatus and medic units and firefighting equipment); issue no more than $7,640,000 of general obligation bonds maturing within 6 years; and levy annual excess property taxes to repay the bonds, all as provided in Resolution No. 26-05.
