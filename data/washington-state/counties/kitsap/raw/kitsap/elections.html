Ballot Resolutions


The Kitsap County Elections Canvassing Board will meet at 9 a.m., Monday, July 17, 2026 for determination of challenged Primary ballots. The board will reconvene at 9 a.m., Tuesday, July 18 to certify the Primary. Both meetings will be at the Kitsap County Elections Processing Center, 8900 SW Imperial Way, Bremerton, WA 98312. Both meetings are open to the public, will be recorded and will be viewable on the Kitsap County Auditor's Elections page, kitsap.gov/elections.

Tabulation System Audit
Before each election we test the ballot tabulation system to make sure it is counting ballots correctly before we begin scanning ballots. This was the first of at least two audits. The second will be the day after election day. This test was July 8.
