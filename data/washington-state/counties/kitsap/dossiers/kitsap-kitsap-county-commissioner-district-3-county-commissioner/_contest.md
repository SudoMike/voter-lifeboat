## What this office does

The three-member county commission adopts budgets and policy and oversees county services, land use, infrastructure, and public health. [S1].

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Katie Walters emphasizes transit, healthcare, permitting, infrastructure, and collaborative governance; Kevin Tisdel emphasizes process discipline, affordability, rural character, and controlled growth. [S1].

## What genuinely differentiates these candidates

- Their submitted records and stated administrative priorities provide the clearest comparison; party preference alone is not used as evidence [S1].
