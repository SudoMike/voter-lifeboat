## What this office does

The elected prosecutor brings criminal cases, represents the county in civil matters, advises county government, and administers the prosecuting attorney's office. [S1].

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Chad Enright emphasizes diversion, treatment courts, race-blind charging, data transparency, staffing, and administrative savings; challenger Joe Lombardi emphasizes violent-crime prosecution, management reform, civil rights, limited diversion, and excluding ICE from county cases. [S1].

## What genuinely differentiates these candidates

- Their submitted records and stated administrative priorities provide the clearest comparison; party preference alone is not used as evidence [S1].
