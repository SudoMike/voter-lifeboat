## What this office does

The clerk maintains Superior Court records, assists court users, and provides filing and passport services. [S1].

## Race dynamics

Incumbent David Lewis offers 41 years in the clerk's office and an e-filing record; deputy clerk Brien Kennedy offers private-sector management experience and proposes public operational audits, plain-language guides, and open office hours. [S1].

## What genuinely differentiates these candidates

- Their submitted records and stated administrative priorities provide the clearest comparison; party preference alone is not used as evidence [S1].
