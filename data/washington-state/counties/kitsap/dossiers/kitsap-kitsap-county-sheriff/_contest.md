## What this office does

The sheriff leads county law enforcement, patrol, investigations, detention-related coordination, emergency response, and agency administration. [S1].

## Race dynamics

Kitsap Sheriff's Office supervisor Brandon Myers emphasizes staffing, retention, professionalism, transparency, and partnerships; deputy and military veteran Rick Kuss emphasizes proactive fentanyl enforcement, constitutional rights, evidence-based policing, and accountability. [S1].

## What genuinely differentiates these candidates

- Their submitted records and stated administrative priorities provide the clearest comparison; party preference alone is not used as evidence [S1].
