## What this office does

The assessor values taxable property and administers valuation appeals and exemption programs; the office does not set tax rates. [S1].

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Phil Cook emphasizes a 12-year administrative record and workflow efficiency; real-estate professional Michael Simonds emphasizes data modernization, neighborhood-level valuation equity, and accessible appeal and exemption tools. [S1].

## What genuinely differentiates these candidates

- Their submitted records and stated administrative priorities provide the clearest comparison; party preference alone is not used as evidence [S1].
