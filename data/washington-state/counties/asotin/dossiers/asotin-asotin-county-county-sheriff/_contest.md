## What this office does

The sheriff leads county law enforcement, operates the county jail, serves court process, and coordinates public-safety functions [S1].

## Race dynamics

The official primary ballot lists Asotin Police Chief Monte Renzelman and Asotin County Undersheriff Blake Richards [S1]. Both have local law-enforcement leadership records, with distinguishable approaches to enforcement, prevention, and community engagement.
