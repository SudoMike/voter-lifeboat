## What it does

Authorizes a one-year excess property-tax levy for maintenance and operation of Clarkston ambulance and emergency medical services in 2027 [S1][S2].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: The levy raises $1,217,828 and requires approximately $1.540 per $1,000 of assessed value, equal to about $154 annually per $100,000 or $770 on $500,000 [S1].

## Arguments for

The official ballot identifies ambulance and EMS maintenance and operations as the use; no committee argument was available in the reviewed official material [S1][S2].

## Arguments against

No committee argument was available; the direct tradeoff is one year of EMS operating revenue versus the excess property-tax levy [S1][S2].

## Axis notes

- A YES vote authorizes a one-year excess property-tax levy for EMS [S1].
