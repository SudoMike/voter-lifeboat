## What it does

Lifts the city fire-service levy lid for two years, adding $0.98 and setting the rate at $1.87 per $1,000. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: At the stated total rate, $187 annually per $100,000 assessed value, or $935 on $500,000; the increase portion is $98 per $100,000. Arithmetic examples are derived from the official rate and are not county model-home estimates [S1].

## Arguments for

The official material identifies the revenue for city fire service; no pro committee statement was available in the archived material [S1].

## Arguments against

No con committee statement was available; the direct trade-off is the named public service funding versus the property-tax levy or benefit charge [S1].
