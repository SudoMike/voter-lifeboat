## What it does

Lifts the district's regular fire levy lid to as much as $1.50 per $1,000 of assessed value. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: At the maximum rate, $150 annually per $100,000 assessed value, or $750 on $500,000. Arithmetic examples are derived from the official rate and are not county model-home estimates [S1].

## Arguments for

The official material identifies the revenue for fire and emergency operations; no pro committee statement was available in the archived material [S1].

## Arguments against

No con committee statement was available; the direct trade-off is the named public service funding versus the property-tax levy or benefit charge [S1].
