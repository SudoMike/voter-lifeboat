## What it does

The proposition authorizes a property-tax levy for fire-protection services in District 6. [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: The official title establishes a fire-service property tax; the reviewed material does not provide a reliable model-home cost estimate. [S1].

## Arguments for

The stated purpose is continued fire-protection service funding. [S1].

## Arguments against

No official con statement was available in the reviewed source; the direct tradeoff is fire-service funding versus property-tax cost. [S1].
