## What this office does

The five-member county council adopts county policy and budgets and oversees services in unincorporated Clark County [S1].

## Race dynamics

Peter Silliman brings county charter and council-office experience; Troy McCoy brings elected municipal and school-board experience [S1]. Both emphasize public safety, affordability, infrastructure, controlled spending, and opposition to taxpayer-funded light rail without voter consent [S1].

## What genuinely differentiates these candidates

- Silliman emphasizes charter checks, budget transparency, and limiting government interference [S1].
- McCoy emphasizes practical municipal governance, managed growth, and open administration [S1].
