## What this office does

The assessor values taxable real property and administers property-tax exemption programs; the office determines value, while taxing districts set levy amounts [S1].

## Race dynamics

Four-term incumbent Peter Van Nortwick faces real-estate investor Tyler Thoune [S1]. Thoune challenges valuation accuracy and appeal outcomes; Van Nortwick emphasizes appraisal credentials, senior exemptions, and a smaller office workforce [S1].

## What genuinely differentiates these candidates

- Thoune emphasizes sale-price alignment, appeals, transparency, and customer service [S1].
- Van Nortwick emphasizes certified appraisal experience, exemption access, technology, and administrative continuity [S1].
