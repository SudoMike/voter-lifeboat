## Background

Van Nortwick says he has served as Clark County Assessor since 2011 and is a certified general appraiser with residential, agricultural, and commercial experience [S1].

## Positions

- Supports expanding senior property-tax exemptions and says the 2026 income threshold rises to $85,000 [S1].
- Supports a state homestead exemption and continued use of technology for equitable, lower-cost administration [S1].

## Record

- Says his office operates with fewer full-time employees than when he was first elected [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Incumbent emphasizing appraisal credentials, exemption administration, and operational continuity [S1].
