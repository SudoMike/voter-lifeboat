## Background

Quiring O'Brien chaired the Clark County Council, represented its District 4, and previously served in both chambers of the Oregon Legislature [S1]. Her professional background includes real estate, appraisal, small-business ownership, and nonprofit management [S1].

## Positions

- She proposes active voter-list maintenance, election safeguards, public communication, fiscal oversight, and searches for waste, fraud, and abuse [S1][S2].
- She says auditor decisions should be nonpartisan and guided by law, ethical standards, and accuracy [S2].

## Scoring notes

- Offers extensive elected executive and legislative experience but no direct election-administration tenure [S1].
- Strongest emphasis on voter-roll verification and auditing for waste or fraud [S1][S2].
