## What the office does

Clark County describes the auditor as the county's chief financial officer and administrator of elections, licensing, recording, and related public services [S1].

## Race dynamics

Deputy Auditor Mitchell Kelly offers direct audit and county-finance experience. Former County Council chair Eileen Quiring O'Brien, state Representative Sharon Wylie, and Vancouver Councilmember Ty Stober emphasize elected management and oversight records [S2].
