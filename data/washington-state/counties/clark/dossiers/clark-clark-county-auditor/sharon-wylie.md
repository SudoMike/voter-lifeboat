## Background

Wylie has represented Washington's 49th Legislative District since 2011 and previously served in Oregon's legislature [S1][S2]. She also reports county government-relations, risk-management, human-resources, city-administration, consulting, and business experience [S1].

## Positions

- She emphasizes safe elections, secure records, fiscal stewardship, modern public-records tools, data privacy, and accountability [S1].
- She says her state legislative and county-operations experience would improve coordination on state-mandated county duties [S1].

## Record

- Her pamphlet cites laws addressing voting rights, county customer service, law-enforcement resources, and civil rights [S1].
