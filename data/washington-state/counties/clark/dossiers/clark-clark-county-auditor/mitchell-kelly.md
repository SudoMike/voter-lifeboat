## Background

Kelly is Clark County's deputy auditor and previously served as reporting and analysis manager and as an assistant Washington state auditor [S1]. County records identify him managing auditor-office financial and grant work [S2][S3].

## Positions

- He promises nonpartisan election administration, financial oversight, operational accuracy, and transparent management [S1].
- He presents his accounting degree, MBA, and 15 years in government finance as preparation for the office [S1].

## Record

- County minutes document his work presenting criminal-justice grant administration to the council [S2].
