## What the office does

The County Council adopts budgets and county policy, including land use, public health, infrastructure, and services in unincorporated Clark County.

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Glen Yung runs on shelter, housing, public-safety, and environmental investments. Dusti Arab emphasizes farmland, housing tools, transit, and services; Lukas Bardue emphasizes fiscally accountable service coordination; Bryan Shull emphasizes taxes, affordability, public safety, and business [S1].
