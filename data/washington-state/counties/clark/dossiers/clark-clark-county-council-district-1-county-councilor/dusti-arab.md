## Background

Arab is an urban farmer, marketer, and parent who operates a backyard plant nursery and local-food projects [S1][S2].

## Positions

- She opposes data centers, detention centers, and expansion of the urban growth boundary [S1].
- She supports community land trusts, cottage housing, easier ADU permitting, public and active transportation, and county partnerships with housing organizations [S1][S2].
- She supports funding and coordination for homelessness services and protection of farmland, water, forests, and green space [S1][S2].

## Scoring notes

- Most restrictive approach to outward development and data centers [S1].
- Combines pro-housing infill tools with public/nonprofit housing partnerships [S1][S2].
