## Background

Yung is the incumbent District 1 councilor and serves on county health, homelessness, mental-health, opioid, clean-water, transit, and disaster-preparedness bodies [S1][S2]. His professional background includes banking, finance, and a family remodeling business [S1].

## Positions

- He supports integrated shelter, addiction and mental-health treatment, affordable-housing preservation, public-safety investment, clean-water work, and senior housing [S1].

## Record

- He reports securing funding for a 120-bed shelter with treatment, protecting mobile-home parks from redevelopment, and advancing senior housing and water-restoration projects [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Only incumbent with a county legislative record [S1][S2].
- Uses public investment and regulatory protection for housing and homelessness outcomes [S1].
