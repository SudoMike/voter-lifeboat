## Background

Matthews is a licensed engineer who reports 44 years in electric utilities, including Georgia Power research and 32 years in varied Bonneville Power Administration assignments [S1].

## Positions

- Supports decarbonization and electrification, expanded solar, partnerships with eastern Washington utilities, and redesigned community-solar programs [S1].
- Says declining snowpack and constrained hydro supply require new resources [S1].

## Endorsements

- Reports endorsement by Washington Conservation Action [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Strongest emphasis in the field on solar expansion and decarbonization [S1].
