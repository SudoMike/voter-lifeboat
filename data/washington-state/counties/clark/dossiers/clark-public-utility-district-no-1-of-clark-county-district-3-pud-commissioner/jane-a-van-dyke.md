## Background

Van Dyke is the incumbent commissioner, an attorney, and an active and former president of the Washington Public Utility Districts Association [S1].

## Positions

- Supports customer service, conservation, efficiency, community and environmental programs, solar, nonprofit rates, and assistance for income-limited customers [S1].

## Record

- Says conservation exceeded regional goals and that the utility had one electric rate increase in 15 years and one water rate increase in 13 years [S1].
- Cites customer-satisfaction and reliable-public-power recognition [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Sole incumbent; emphasizes continuity, conservation, assistance, and rate history [S1].
