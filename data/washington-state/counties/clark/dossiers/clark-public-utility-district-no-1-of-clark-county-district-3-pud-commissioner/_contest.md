## What this office does

The elected commissioner governs the public utility's electric and water policy, rates, resource planning, reliability, conservation, and infrastructure [S1].

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Jane Van Dyke faces two candidates with long utility careers: engineer Gordon Matthews and utility worker Kevin Roegner [S1]. The central tradeoffs concern decarbonization and resource acquisition, rate stability, reliability, grid modernization, and conservation [S1].

## What genuinely differentiates these candidates

- Matthews proposes expanded solar partnerships and a faster clean-energy transition [S1].
- Roegner foregrounds protection systems, cybersecurity, storm resilience, and strict financial oversight [S1].
- Van Dyke foregrounds the utility's conservation, rate, reliability, and assistance record [S1].
