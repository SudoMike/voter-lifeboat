## What this office does

The clerk is the Superior Court's administrative and financial officer, preserving case records and managing case-related deposits and disbursements [S1].

## Race dynamics

Four-term incumbent Scott Weber faces mediator and attorney Rachel Shapiro and longtime attorney Gerald Gray [S1]. Weber emphasizes continuity and court technology, Shapiro emphasizes accessibility and transparency, and Gray emphasizes courtroom experience [S1].

## What genuinely differentiates these candidates

- Weber offers a 16-year administrative record [S1].
- Shapiro proposes monthly public office hours and easier online financial access [S1].
- Gray emphasizes 25 years of prosecution and defense experience [S1].
