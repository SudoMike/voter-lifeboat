## What it does

Proposition 6 renews East County Fire & Rescue's emergency-medical-services property-tax levy for six years, collected beginning in 2027, at no more than $0.35 per $1,000 of assessed value [S1]. The current voter-approved levy expires at the end of 2026 [S1].

Cost line: up to $140 per year on $400,000 assessed value, $227.50 on $650,000, or $350 on $1 million [S1].

## Arguments for

The submitted pro committee says renewal maintains EMS funding for paramedics, EMTs, ambulances, equipment, and training and continues rather than creates the levy [S1].

## Arguments against

No person volunteered to submit a con statement or rebuttal, so the official pamphlet contains no organized opposition argument [S1].

## Axis notes

- Taxes versus emergency-service continuity: a yes vote continues a dedicated property levy; a no vote lets it expire [S1].
- The record is one-sided because no con statement was submitted [S1].
