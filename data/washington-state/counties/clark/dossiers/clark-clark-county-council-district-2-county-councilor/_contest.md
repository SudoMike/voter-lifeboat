## What this office does

The county council adopts policy and budgets and oversees county services, land use, infrastructure, and public health [S1].

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Michelle Belkot faces public administrator Martin Pittioni and teacher John Zingale [S1]. Affordability, growth, infrastructure, safety, and public services recur across the field, with sharper differences on taxes, transit, annexation, and social investment [S1].

## What genuinely differentiates these candidates

- Pittioni emphasizes bipartisan administration, balanced budgets, behavioral health, and consensus [S1].
- Belkot emphasizes limiting taxes and spending, opposing annexation and light rail subsidies, and prioritizing police and infrastructure [S1].
- Zingale emphasizes social services, affordable housing, natural-space protection, labor, and rights [S1].
