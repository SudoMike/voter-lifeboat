## Background

Zingale is a middle-school history teacher, a union representative, and a member of the Clark County Historic Preservation Commission [S1].

## Positions

- Supports infrastructure, social-service investment, affordable housing, natural-space protection, working families, and fundamental rights [S1].
- Says county government should be responsive and avoid wasting taxpayer money [S1].

## Endorsements

- Reports endorsements from State Senator Cortes, Vancouver Mayor Anne McEnerny-Ogle, and County Councilor Fuentes [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Most explicit candidate on social services, labor, rights, and conservation alongside housing [S1].
