## Background

Belkot is the incumbent District 2 councilor and reports more than 20 years in federal acquisition and procurement plus 25 years of Air Force and Navy service [S1].

## Positions

- Opposes new taxes she considers unnecessary and prioritizes law enforcement, fire protection, and infrastructure before program expansion [S1].
- Opposes unwanted Vancouver annexation, taxpayer subsidies for Oregon light rail, and tolls tied to the proposed interstate bridge approach [S1].
- Supports reducing housing costs and taxes [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Most explicit candidate on tax restraint, annexation, and light rail [S1].
