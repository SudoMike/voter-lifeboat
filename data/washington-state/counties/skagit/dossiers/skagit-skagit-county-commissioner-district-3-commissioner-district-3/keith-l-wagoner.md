## Background

Wagoner has served in the state Senate since 2018 after terms as Sedro-Woolley mayor and councilmember; he also reports a 23-year Navy and Marine Corps career [S1].

## Positions

- Supports farms and businesses and reducing regulatory and tax burdens [S1].
- Emphasizes rural representation, infrastructure funding, and pragmatic administration [S1].
