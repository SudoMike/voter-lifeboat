## What this office does

County commissioners adopt the county budget and policy, oversee departments and infrastructure, and administer land-use, health, safety, and human-service functions [S1].

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Joe Burns emphasizes affordability and current county projects; Connie Krier runs on independent oversight; state senator and former mayor Keith Wagoner emphasizes regulatory and tax relief [S1].
