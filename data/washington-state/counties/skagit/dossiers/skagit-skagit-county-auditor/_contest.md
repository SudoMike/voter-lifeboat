## What this office does

The auditor administers elections and vote-by-mail, maintains public records, manages licensing, and oversees county financial processes [S1].

## Race dynamics

Jeremy Harrison-Smith emphasizes election access and administrative modernization; former councilmember Mark Hulst emphasizes management experience, legal compliance, and nonpartisan administration [S1].
