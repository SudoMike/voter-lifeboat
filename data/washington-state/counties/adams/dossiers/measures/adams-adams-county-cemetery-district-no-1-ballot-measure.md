## What it does

Authorizes a one-year maintenance and operations property-tax levy for cemetery upkeep, collected in 2027 [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Raises $55,500 at approximately $0.55 per $1,000, or about $275 on $500,000 [S1].

## Arguments for

Approval funds one year of cemetery maintenance and operations; no committee statement was available [S1].

## Arguments against

Rejection avoids the one-year levy; no committee statement was available [S1].

- Cemetery upkeep versus a one-year property-tax charge [S1].
