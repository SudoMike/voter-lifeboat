## What this office does

The auditor administers elections, licensing, recording, and county financial functions.

## Race dynamics

Courtney Fannin, elections administrator Jacque Laird, and Stephanie Dormaier seek to replace the retiring auditor. The available source establishes direct office experience for Laird but only candidacy for Fannin and Dormaier.
