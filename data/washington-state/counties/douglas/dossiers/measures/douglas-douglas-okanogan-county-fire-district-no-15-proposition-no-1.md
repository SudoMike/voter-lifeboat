## What it does

Continues funding emergency medical services through a regular property-tax levy of up to $0.50 per $1,000 for six consecutive years beginning with 2027 collections [S1][S2].

## Fiscal mechanics

At the maximum rate, the levy is $50 annually per $100,000 of assessed value, or $250 on $500,000 [S1].

## Arguments for

The official material identifies continued emergency-medical-service funding as the purpose; no committee statement was available [S1].

## Arguments against

No official opposition statement was available; the tradeoff is continued EMS funding against the six-year property-tax levy [S1].

## Axis notes

- Continued property-tax levy versus continued local EMS funding [S1].
