## What it does

Renews a one-year excess levy raising $1,460,000 for maintaining, developing, and expanding district health-care facilities and services [S1][S2].

## Fiscal mechanics

The estimated rate is $0.33 per $1,000 of assessed value for 2027 collection: $33 annually per $100,000, or $165 on $500,000 [S1].

## Arguments for

The official title says the levy maintains availability of essential health-care services and funds maintenance, development, and expansion [S1].

## Arguments against

No official opposition statement was available; the direct tradeoff is the health-care funding against a one-year excess property-tax levy [S1].

- Property-tax renewal versus health-care facility and service investment [S1].
