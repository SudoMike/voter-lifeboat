## What this office does

The elected prosecutor brings criminal cases, represents the county in civil matters, advises county government, and manages charging, diversion, victim-service, and office-administration policy.

## Race dynamics

Four attorneys compete for the open office: civil deputy Jesse Corkern emphasizes consistent standards and management reform; chief criminal deputy Erik Sigmar emphasizes prosecution leadership plus diversion and rehabilitation; defense attorney and school-board president Ryan Swinburnson emphasizes balanced experience and trust; assistant chief criminal deputy Dona Bracke emphasizes serious-case prosecution, victims, and law-enforcement partnership.
