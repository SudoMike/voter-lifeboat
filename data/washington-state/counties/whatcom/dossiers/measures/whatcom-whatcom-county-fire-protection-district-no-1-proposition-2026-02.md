## What it does

Restores the district's regular fire levy to $1.48 per $1,000 and authorizes a 106% limit factor for nine years. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: At the stated maximum, $148 annually per $100,000 assessed value, or $740 on $500,000. This arithmetic is derived from the official rate, not a county model-home estimate [S1].

## Arguments for

The official election material identifies the revenue as local fire-protection funding; no pro committee statement was available. [S1]

## Arguments against

No con committee statement was available; the direct trade-off is higher and growing property-tax revenue versus taxpayer restraint. [S1]
