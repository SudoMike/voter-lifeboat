## What it does

Restores the cross-county hospital district's regular levy to $0.50 per $1,000 for 2027 collection and resets the later levy base. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: At the stated rate, $50 annually per $100,000 assessed value, or $250 on $500,000. This arithmetic is derived from the official rate, not a county model-home estimate [S1].

## Arguments for

The official election material identifies the levy as hospital-district funding; no pro committee statement was available. [S1]

## Arguments against

No con committee statement was available; the direct trade-off is public-health funding versus higher property tax. [S1]
