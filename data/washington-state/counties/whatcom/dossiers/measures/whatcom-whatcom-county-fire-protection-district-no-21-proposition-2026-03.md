## What it does

Sets the regular fire levy at $1.20 per $1,000 and authorizes a 103% limit factor for five years. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: At the stated rate, $120 annually per $100,000 assessed value, or $600 on $500,000. This arithmetic is derived from the official rate, not a county model-home estimate [S1].

## Arguments for

The official election material identifies the levy as local fire-protection funding; no pro committee statement was available. [S1]

## Arguments against

No con committee statement was available; the direct trade-off is fire-service revenue versus higher property tax. [S1]
