## Background

Paul Burrill has more than 30 years in commercial fishing, owns seafood and trucking businesses, and has chaired the port marina advisory committee since 2018 [S1][S2][S3].

## Positions

- He calls for disciplined, accountable port management, working-waterfront investment, airport and cargo development, and job creation [S2][S3].

## Scoring notes

- His differentiation is marine-business ownership and direct port advisory experience [S2][S3].
