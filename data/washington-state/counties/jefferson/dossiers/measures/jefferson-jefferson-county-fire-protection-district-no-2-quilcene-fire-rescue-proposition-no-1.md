## What it does

Restores the regular fire levy to $1.25 per $1,000 for fire, EMS, apparatus, equipment, staffing, and firefighter safety. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: $125 annually per $100,000, or $625 on $500,000; the current rate is approximately $0.75. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says restored funding supports staffing, equipment, training, and emergency response. [S1]

## Arguments against

No against committee formed; the tradeoff is restored emergency-service funding against higher property tax. [S1]

## Axis notes

- Property-tax level versus the specified local service funding [S1].
