## What it does

Restores the fire levy to as much as $1.50 per $1,000 and permits a 104% limit factor for the following nine years. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Initially up to $150 annually per $100,000, or $750 on $500,000; later collections may grow 4% annually. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says stable funding supports staffing, training, equipment, planning, and development of advanced life support. [S1]

## Arguments against

No against committee formed; the tradeoff is long-term emergency-service stability against a restored and growing levy. [S1]
