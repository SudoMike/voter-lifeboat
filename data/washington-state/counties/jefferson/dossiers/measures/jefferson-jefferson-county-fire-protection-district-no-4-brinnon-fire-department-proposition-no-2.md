## What it does

Restores the dedicated EMS levy to as much as $0.50 per $1,000 and permits a 104% limit factor for nine following years. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Initially up to $50 annually per $100,000, or $250 on $500,000; later collections may grow 4% annually. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says dedicated funding supports EMS supplies, training, vehicles, response, and advanced life support. [S1]

## Arguments against

No against committee formed; the tradeoff is long-term EMS funding against a restored and growing levy. [S1]
