## What it does

Provides six years of dedicated 2027-2032 funding for parks, recreation, six community centers, the fairgrounds, and 4-H. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: $0.21 per $1,000, or $21 annually per $100,000 and $105 on $500,000. [S1]

## Arguments for

The county says the levy replaces unavailable funding for community programs and facilities. [S1]

## Arguments against

No official opposition statement was available; the tradeoff is dedicated services against a new six-year property-tax charge. [S1]
