## What it does

Restores the cemetery levy to $0.04 per $1,000 for maintenance, repairs, capital costs, and administration. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: $4 annually per $100,000, or $20 on $500,000; the county estimates a $6.40 annual increase on $400,000. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says restoration maintains cemetery grounds and facilities. [S1]

## Arguments against

No against committee formed; the tradeoff is cemetery services against the levy increase. [S1]

## Axis notes

- Property-tax level versus the specified local service funding [S1].
