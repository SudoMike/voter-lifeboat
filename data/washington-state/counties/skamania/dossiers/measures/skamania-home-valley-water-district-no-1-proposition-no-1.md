## What it does

Authorizes a one-year excess levy replacing an expiring levy for water-system maintenance and capital improvements [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: $25,000 for 2027, approximately $0.57 per $1,000 assessed value, or $285 on $500,000 [S1].
