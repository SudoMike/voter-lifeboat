## What it does

Restores the EMS property-tax levy from about $0.36 to $0.50 per $1,000 for 2027 and 2028 and resets the future levy base. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: The pro committee estimates under $7 per month, about $80 per year, for an average $571,000 Everett property. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says EMS represents nearly 82% of fire responses and restoration maintains paramedic and EMT service. [S1]

## Arguments against

No con statement was submitted after recruitment. [S1]

## Axis notes

- Rate restoration and permanent base reset trade higher property tax for current EMS service. [S1]
