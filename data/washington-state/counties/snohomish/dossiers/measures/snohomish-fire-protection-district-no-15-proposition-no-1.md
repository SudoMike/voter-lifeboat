## What it does

Authorizes a four-year excess property-tax levy collecting $450,000 annually to maintain fire, EMS, and rescue service. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Estimated rates range from $0.6214 per $1,000 in 2027 to $0.6031 in 2030. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says stable funds preserve staffing, equipment, response times, and existing service. [S1]

## Arguments against

The con committee opposes additional taxes and asks the district to pursue efficiencies or alternatives. [S1]

## Axis notes

- Emergency-service continuity versus additional property tax. [S1]
