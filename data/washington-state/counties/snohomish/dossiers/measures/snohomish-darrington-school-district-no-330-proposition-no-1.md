## What it does

Replaces an expiring four-year school levy, collecting $950,000 annually for class size, counselors, arts, athletics, music, transportation, staffing, supplies, and operations. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Estimated rates decline from $1.24 per $1,000 in 2027 to $1.02 in 2030. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee calls it a no-increase replacement needed for programs and staff. [S1]

## Arguments against

The con committee argues spending and academic outcomes are unsustainable and urges prioritization before renewal. [S1]

## Axis notes

- School services and staffing versus tax and performance accountability. [S1]
