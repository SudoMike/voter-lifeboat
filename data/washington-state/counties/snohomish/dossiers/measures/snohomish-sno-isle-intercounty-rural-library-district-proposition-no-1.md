## What it does

Raises the library levy from about $0.304 to $0.47 per $1,000 in 2027, resets the future base, and funds services, staffing, materials, technology, mobile service, and facilities. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Estimated increase $124.25 yearly or $10.35 monthly for a $748,100 home. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says the levy sustains and expands learning, access, collections, staffing, mobile service, and safe facilities. [S1]

## Arguments against

The con committee calls it a 55% tax increase and argues spending has outpaced inflation and priorities should be narrowed. [S1]

## Axis notes

- Expanded library services versus tax and spending restraint. [S1]
