## What it does

Restores the permanent EMS levy to $0.50 per $1,000 for 2027 collections and permits CPI-based growth for five following years. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: No model-home cost is stated in the pamphlet. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says restoration maintains personnel, vehicles, equipment, training, response times, and service stability. [S1]

## Arguments against

No con statement was submitted after recruitment. [S1]

## Axis notes

- Inflation-linked EMS revenue versus higher property taxes. [S1]
