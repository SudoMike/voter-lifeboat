## What it does

Creates a 0.1% sales and use tax for current and enhanced police, dispatch, court, prosecution, defense, jail, and support services. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: One dollar per $1,000 of taxable retail purchases; estimated to raise about $300,000 in 2027. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says growth and rising contracted-service costs require dedicated revenue and that visitors also contribute. [S1]

## Arguments against

No con statement was submitted after recruitment. [S1]

## Axis notes

- New sales tax and enforcement/service funding. [S1]
