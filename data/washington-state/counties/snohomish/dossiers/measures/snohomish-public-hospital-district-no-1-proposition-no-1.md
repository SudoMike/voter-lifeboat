## What it does

Authorizes up to $382 million in 30-year bonds and excess property taxes to construct and equip a replacement EvergreenHealth Monroe hospital. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Estimated average $0.437 per $1,000, about $40.75 monthly for a $745,000 home. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee cites aging, overcrowding, capacity, technology, local access, audits, and oversight. [S1]

## Arguments against

No con statement was submitted after recruitment. [S1]

## Axis notes

- Large long-term capital levy versus replacement hospital capacity. [S1]
