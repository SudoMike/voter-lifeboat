## What it does

Restores the EMS levy from about $0.32 to $0.50 per $1,000 in 2027, permits up to 6% annual growth through 2032, and resets the later base. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Rate increase of about $0.18 per $1,000 in 2027; no model-home cost is stated. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says EMS exceeds 80% of calls and current revenue does not meet operating costs. [S1]

## Arguments against

No con statement was submitted after recruitment. [S1]

## Axis notes

- EMS funding versus a multi-year property-tax lid lift. [S1]
