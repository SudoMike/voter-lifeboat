# Yakima County Commissioner, District 1

County commissioners adopt the budget and policy and oversee county services, land use, infrastructure, and public health. VoteWA lists Roman Buermann, Robert Udell, Dave Mullen, and Kristoffer Strutner [VoteWA](https://voter.votewa.gov/CandidateList.aspx?c=39&e=898); Mullen suspended his campaign after the withdrawal deadline and remains on the ballot [NonStop Local](https://www.nbcrightnow.com/news/yakima-county-commissioner-race-shifts-as-dave-mullen-drops-out/article_7a4d65ce-7e44-42a7-87bc-da4ca1ec6384.html).
