## Background

Udell has 36 years in Yakima County law enforcement and has served as sheriff since 2018; he also has a horticulture degree and fruit-industry background [S2][S3].

## Positions

- Prioritizes public safety, agriculture, water rights, budget management, accountable leadership, and cooperation among agencies and cities [S2][S3].
- Presents his sheriff-office budgeting and relationship-building as preparation for countywide administration [S2][S3].
