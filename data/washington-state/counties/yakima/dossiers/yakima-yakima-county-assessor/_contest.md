# Yakima County Assessor

The assessor inventories and values taxable property and administers property-tax exemptions; taxing districts, not the assessor, set levy amounts [Yakima County Assessor](https://www.yakimacounty.us/151/Assessor). The official 2026 candidate list shows Jacob Tate and Keith Effler, both preferring the Republican Party [VoteWA](https://voter.votewa.gov/CandidateList.aspx?c=39&e=898).
