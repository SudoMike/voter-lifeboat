## Background

Patterson is the county treasurer office's senior manager for investments and banking, a position local reporting says she has held since 2014 [S3]. VoteWA confirms her Republican candidacy [S1].

## Positions

- She advocates better protection of financial data, upgraded software, clearer taxpayer reporting, and modernized investment and tax-collection systems [S2].
- She calls for stronger internal controls, staff cross-training, and centralized receipting [S2][S3].

## Record

Her current role supplies direct experience with two core treasurer functions: county investments and banking [S3].

## Scoring notes

- Substantial office-specific management experience [S3].
- Platform focuses on modernization, controls, resilience, and reporting [S2].
