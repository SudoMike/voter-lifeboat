# Yakima County Treasurer

The treasurer collects and distributes property taxes, manages county cash and investments, and serves as banker for county government and local taxing districts [Yakima County Treasurer](https://www.yakimacounty.us/181/Treasurer). VoteWA lists Stephanie Patterson, preferring the Republican Party, and Sam Johnson, preferring the Independent Party [VoteWA](https://voter.votewa.gov/CandidateList.aspx?c=39&e=898).
