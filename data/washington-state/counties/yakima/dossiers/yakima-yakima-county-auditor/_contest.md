# Yakima County Auditor

The auditor administers elections, records property documents, issues licenses and passports, and performs county financial functions [Yakima County Auditor](https://www.yakimacounty.us/160/Auditor). VoteWA lists Maria Cuevas, Charles Ross, and Matt Barker [VoteWA](https://voter.votewa.gov/CandidateList.aspx?c=39&e=898).
