## Background

Cuevas is a longtime Yakima Valley College educator and community-board member with graduate training in public health and social welfare [S2][S3]. VoteWA confirms her Democratic candidacy [S1].

## Positions

- Proposes precinct-level signature-challenge reporting, bilingual voter education, and consistent urban and rural election standards [S2][S3].
- Proposes plain-language bilingual financial reports, a public expenditure dashboard, and a first-year operational review [S2][S3].
- Supports online appointments, digital records, wait-time targets, bilingual customer staffing, and plain-language service guides [S2][S3].

## Scoring notes

- Access, transparency, audit, and modernization agenda; no auditor-office management record documented [S2][S3].
