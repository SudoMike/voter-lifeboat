## Background

Haueter describes himself as an Air Force veteran, former Yakima County special-assault prosecutor, and current Kennewick assistant city attorney [S2]. VoteWA confirms his Republican candidacy [S1].

## Positions

- He prioritizes gang enforcement, prosecution of repeat violent offenders, and stronger victim services [S2].
- He calls for close coordination with law enforcement and presents public safety as the office's central mission [S2].

## Record

His campaign cites convictions obtained while prosecuting sexual assault and child-abuse cases in Yakima County [S2]. Those performance claims are candidate-supplied and were not independently audited here.

## Scoring notes

- Direct prosecutorial and municipal-law experience [S2].
- Clear enforcement-oriented agenda, with less detail on civil counsel and diversion functions [S2].
