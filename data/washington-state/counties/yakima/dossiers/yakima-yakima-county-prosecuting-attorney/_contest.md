# Yakima County Prosecuting Attorney

The prosecutor brings felony and juvenile cases for the state, advises county government, and represents the county in civil matters [Yakima County Prosecuting Attorney](https://www.yakimacounty.us/190/Prosecuting-Attorney). VoteWA lists Quinn Dalan and Tyler Haueter, both preferring the Republican Party [VoteWA](https://voter.votewa.gov/CandidateList.aspx?c=39&e=898).
