## Background

Dalan says he has lived in Yakima for nearly two decades and has worked as a deputy prosecutor, assigned-counsel defense attorney, civil lawyer, and pro tem judge [S2]. VoteWA confirms his Republican candidacy [S1].

## Positions

- He frames the office around fair application of law, accountability, and public trust [S2].
- He emphasizes experience on both prosecution and defense sides of criminal cases [S2].

## Record

His campaign cites work with the YWCA and Central Washington Legal Aid in addition to courtroom and civil practice [S2]. These claims are candidate-supplied and were not independently verified in the reviewed source set.

## Scoring notes

- Broad legal perspective spanning prosecution, defense, civil work, and judging [S2].
- Platform is principle-oriented and offers limited operational detail [S2].
