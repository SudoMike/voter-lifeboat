## Background

Church says he has 28 years in law enforcement, including 16 years with Toppenish Police and service with the sheriff's office since 2016; he is a lieutenant overseeing standards, training, and policy [S2]. Local reporting corroborates his role and Sheriff Bob Udell's endorsement [S3].
