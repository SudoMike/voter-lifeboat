## What this office does

The clerk maintains Superior Court records, receives filings and payments, supports court operations, and provides public access to case records [S1].

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Staci Myklebust faces Ashley White; public records establish Myklebust's court-administration tenure, while the approved source set contains little substantive information about White [S1].
