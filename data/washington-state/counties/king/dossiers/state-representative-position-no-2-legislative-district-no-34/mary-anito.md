## Background
Mary Anito (Prefers Democratic Party) is a first-time candidate challenging House Majority Leader Joe Fitzgibbon for 34th LD Position 2; The Washington Observer describes her as a political newcomer [S1][S4]. Her professional background is in operations and supply-chain management: roles at REI (senior manager, operations; retirement plan committee fiduciary), Wayfair, Caterpillar, Cisco Systems, Hum Nutrition, and Shelf Engine, plus home renovation work through Jenito Homes in Seattle [S1]. Education: BS in Materials Science and Engineering, Johns Hopkins University; MBA and MS as an MIT Leaders for Global Operations Fellow [S1]. Community service includes board roles (secretary/treasurer) at ROOTS Young Adult Shelter and co-op preschools, and volunteering with Habitat for Humanity and Junior Achievement [S1].

## Positions
- Fiscal approach: argues "fiscal responsibility and strong public investment are not mutually exclusive"; wants to balance the budget while funding infrastructure, education, and healthcare [S1].
