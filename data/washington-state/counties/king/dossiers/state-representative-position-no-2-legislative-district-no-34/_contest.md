# State Representative Position No. 2, Legislative District No. 34 — contest overview

- depth: light
- All candidates advance: yes (two candidates in a top-two primary; both proceed to the general regardless of primary result).

State representatives serve two-year terms; the 34th LD covers West Seattle, Vashon Island, White Center, and west Burien. This race pits 15-year incumbent and House Majority Leader Joe Fitzgibbon against political newcomer Mary Anito; both state a Democratic Party preference. The visible differentiating axes are experience/insider status (legislative leadership and a long climate/tax record vs. private-sector operations management) and fiscal emphasis (Fitzgibbon's progressive-revenue record vs. Anito's "fiscal responsibility plus public investment" framing). Anito's platform detail is limited — her website was not retrievable — so evidence asymmetry between the candidates is itself a feature of this contest.
