# State Senator, Legislative District No. 34 — contest overview

- depth: light
- All candidates advance: yes (single candidate; top-two primary sends up to two candidates to the general regardless).

State senators serve in Olympia writing state law and budgets; the 34th LD covers West Seattle, Vashon Island, White Center, and west Burien. This seat opened when Sen. Joe Nguyen resigned in January 2025 to lead the state Department of Commerce; the King County Council appointed then-Rep. Emily Alvarado to the seat, and she is the only candidate filed. With no opponent, no issue axes differentiate candidates in this race; the dossier documents Alvarado's record and platform for voter information only.
