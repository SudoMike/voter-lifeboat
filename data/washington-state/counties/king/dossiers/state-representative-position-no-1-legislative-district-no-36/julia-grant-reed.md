## Background
Julia Grant Reed (Prefers Democratic Party) is the incumbent State Representative for the 36th Legislative District, Position 1, and a Seattle native seeking re-election [S1][S2]. She works as a consultant at Kinetic West on strategic planning for workforce and youth apprenticeship programs [S1]. Previously she was Senior Policy Advisor & Regional Affairs Director for the City of Seattle (youth employment, workforce, transit, arts policy) and served in the Obama administration at the US State Department and Office of Management and Budget [S1]. The daughter of public school educators, she is a parishioner at St. Anne Catholic Church and a past board member of the YMCA Social Impact Center, Global Leadership Forum, and Fuse WA [S1]. Education: MPA, Princeton University; BA, Smith College [S1].

## Positions
- Workforce/youth: says she will build on her expertise in youth and workforce development, investing in apprenticeship pathways and higher education [S1].
- Housing: says she will advance pro-housing policies to meet Seattle's urgent and long-term needs [S1].
- Economy/arts: wants to expand Washington's creative economy and support the maritime economy [S1].
- Transportation/climate: pledges to be a strong voice for climate-friendly transportation solutions [S1].
