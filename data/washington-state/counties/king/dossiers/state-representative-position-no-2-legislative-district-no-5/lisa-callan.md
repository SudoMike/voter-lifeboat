## Background
Incumbent Democratic state representative for the 5th Legislative District, seeking re-election in 2026 [S1][S2]. Vice-Chair of the House Capital Budget Committee; member of the House Appropriations and K-12 Education committees; previously served on Human Services & Early Learning [S1]. Former Issaquah School Board director [S1]. Former Boeing lead engineer/project manager and software program management consultant [S1][S2]. BS in mathematics (computer science emphasis), Northern Arizona University [S1]. Community involvement includes Kiwanis, Issaquah Chamber of Commerce, PTA leadership, and past boards of The Garage Teen Center and Influence the Choice [S1]. The 5th LD includes Snoqualmie, North Bend, Issaquah, Maple Valley, Black Diamond, Enumclaw, Fall City, and parts of Sammamish [S2].

## Positions
- Education: public school funding and mental health care for kids [S1].
- Housing: "workforce housing for the families who serve our communities" [S1].
- Gun policy: supports gun-safety measures [S1].
- Reproductive rights: says she will defend reproductive freedom [S1].
- Childcare: fight for affordable childcare [S1].
- Environment: protect forests and clean air [S1].
- Taxes: wants "a tax code that doesn't squeeze working people" [S1].

## Endorsements
- Pamphlet lists: educators, State Labor Council, grocery workers, firefighters, nurses, Sierra Club, Washington Conservation Action, Planned Parenthood, Alliance for Gun Responsibility, building trades, Housing Alliance, Congresswoman Kim Schrier, and local mayors/councilmembers [S1].

## Scoring notes
- Multi-term incumbent with budget-committee leadership (Capital Budget vice-chair, Appropriations member); opponent is a first-time candidate [S1].
- Engineering/school-board background frames her education and budgeting emphasis [S1].
- Broad institutional endorsement list (labor, environmental, reproductive-rights groups) versus opponent's party-only endorsements [S1].
- Two-candidate race; both advance to the general regardless of primary result.
