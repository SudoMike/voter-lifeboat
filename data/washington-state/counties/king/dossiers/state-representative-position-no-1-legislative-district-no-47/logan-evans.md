## Background

Logan Evans (PDC filer name: Ryan Logan Evans) is a first-time legislative candidate running under the "Cascade Democrat Party" label [S1][S3]. He is a public-school teacher who has taught in both the Kent and Auburn school districts and currently teaches students who are struggling to stay in school [S1]; his campaign describes him as born in Renton, raised in Kent, and teaching at an alternative public high school in Auburn [S2]. He graduated from Auburn Mountainview High School, holds a bachelor's degree from Western Washington University and a master's from Western Governors University [S1]. He volunteers with the Covington Rotary Club, Kent and Covington Chambers of Commerce, Kent Senior Activity Center, Kent Cornucopia Days, The Hope Factory (building housing for the homeless), and Battlefield Addiction (addiction-recovery support) [S1]. He lost a Kent City Council primary in August 2025 [S4].

## Positions

- **"Cascade Democrat" framing:** Defines it as valuing "actionable rational solutions that don't waste your tax dollars" [S1]; a Democrat who "puts results first," pairing compassion with competence, and calls himself a "rational radical" [S2].
- **Housing:** Supports high-rise housing near light rail to lower housing costs [S1].
- **Homelessness/addiction:** Proposes a "three-strikes" policy with six months of mandatory rehab "to help people off the streets and into successful treatment" [S1].
- **Affordability/spending:** Focused on lowering costs and efficient use of tax dollars [S1]; wants South King County "more affordable, safer, and better governed," centered on working families [S2].
