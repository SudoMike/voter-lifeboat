## Background
Yuri Marinchik is a first-time candidate (no elected experience listed) who describes himself as a working-class immigrant who works full-time while building a small business with his wife [S1]. He reports no formal elected, professional, or educational credentials beyond hands-on small-business and working experience [S1]. His campaign website offers a general message about integrity and community-focused representation but few biographical details [S2].

## Positions
- **Affordability:** says he will fight for lower housing and energy costs and "fair taxes" [S1].
- **Public safety:** calls for stronger public safety, without listed specifics [S1].
- **Regulation / role of government:** criticizes "endless rules that make life harder and increase government control" and frames his candidacy against "career politicians" [S1].
- **Term limits:** signed the U.S. Term Limits pledge to support a congressional term-limits convention [S4].
