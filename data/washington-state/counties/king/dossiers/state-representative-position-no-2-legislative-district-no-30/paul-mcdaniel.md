## Background
Paul McDaniel (prefers Independent Party) is a former Federal Way City Councilmember, appointed by the council to Position 2 [S1][S3]. He owns 7th Power, Inc., a technology consulting business, and is a U.S. Marine Corps veteran of the Iraq War [S1]. He attended Federal Way High School and Highline College [S1]. He volunteered at St. Francis Hospital for nine years, coached middle-school wrestling, and teaches Jiu-Jitsu [S1]. He ran for his council seat in the August 2025 primary but was not among the two candidates who advanced to that general election [S4].

## Positions
- **Taxes/affordability:** says rising housing, food, gas, and energy costs are hurting working families and that he will reduce taxes [S1].
- **Public safety:** will work to improve public safety; website says he would empower law enforcement with necessary tools [S1][S2].
- **Housing/jobs:** expand affordable housing and family-wage jobs; website emphasizes removing barriers to "middle housing" [S1][S2].
- **Fiscal accountability:** frames Olympia as marked by "fraud, corruption, and weak leadership"; website stresses better management of existing government resources [S1][S2].
- **Girls' sports:** says he will "protect girls' sports" [S1].

## Endorsements
None listed in the pamphlet or found on the campaign website [S1][S2].
