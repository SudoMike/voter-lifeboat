## Background
John Bielka (prefers Democrat Party) is a first-time candidate ("first time public servant") [S1]. He is a civil engineer and public works director with more than 30 years managing infrastructure, budgets, and operations; he has owned an excavation business and raises cattle on his farm outside Orting [S1]. He holds a civil engineering degree from the University of Saskatchewan with graduate coursework in engineering economics and accounting [S1]. He volunteers as a Career and Technical Education advisor at Chief Leschi Schools and writes grants for the Orting Senior Center and Orting Food Bank [S1].

## Positions
- **Affordability/fiscal:** will "work across the aisle to lower everyday costs"; website emphasizes responsible budgeting and reduced unnecessary regulation [S1][S2].
- **Infrastructure:** improve roads and utilities; website stresses faster delivery of transportation, housing, and flood-protection projects [S1][S2].
- **Education/trades:** expand career and technical education, including trades pathways [S1].
- **Public safety:** supports adequate police staffing [S1][S2].
- **Gun policy:** website states support for the Second Amendment and responsible gun ownership [S2].
- **Accountability:** campaign tagline "Results, not rhetoric"; calls for bill-reading, "transparent math," and taxpayer protection [S2].

## Endorsements
None listed in the pamphlet or on the campaign website as of 2026-07-16 [S1][S2].

## Scoring notes
- The only Democrat in the race; positions himself as a pragmatic, cross-aisle problem-solver rather than a party-line candidate (e.g., explicit Second Amendment support) [S1][S2].
- Deep technical infrastructure/budget background but no elected or legislative record, against an incumbent former mayor [S1].
- Both candidates advance to the general regardless of primary outcome.
