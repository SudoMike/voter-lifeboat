## What this office does

One of two House seats for LD 43 (central Seattle: Capitol Hill, University
District, Wallingford, Fremont); votes on state law, budget, and taxes.

## Race dynamics

Single-candidate race: incumbent Rep. Shaun Scott is the only candidate on
the primary ballot and advances to the general automatically. Research
depth is light accordingly.

- **Shaun Scott** (Prefers Democratic Party) — first-term incumbent elected
  November 2024; Seattle DSA member and the first open socialist in the
  Washington Legislature since the 1910s; Vice Chair of the House Labor &
  Workplace Standards Committee.

## Differentiating axes

- Uncontested, so no intra-race differentiation. For rubric purposes, the
  relevant material is his record (corporate tax-break repeal work,
  document recording fee parity for large financial institutions, wildfire
  response funding, striking-worker unemployment benefits) and his
  democratic-socialist framing.
