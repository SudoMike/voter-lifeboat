## Background
Republican-preference first-time candidate from Federal Way; no elected experience listed [S1][S2]. Small business owner with experience in budgeting, marketing, and customer service; background in health and wellness with training in massage therapy, herbalism, and yoga instruction [S1]. Community involvement includes her daughter's co-op, nonprofit fundraising benefiting Seattle Children's Hospital, church volunteering, and childcare/postpartum support for local families [S1].

## Positions
- Affordability/taxes: opposes new taxes on working families and businesses; says government spending growth is driving up property taxes, gas prices, and utility bills [S1][S2].
- Small business: cites rising B&O taxes, payroll costs, and regulatory/compliance burdens as threats to business survival; wants to reduce barriers [S2].
- Government accountability: calls for transparent tracking of taxpayer funds and program performance audits, citing untracked childcare funding and grant-oversight gaps [S2].
- Public safety: proposes fully staffing police departments, holding repeat offenders accountable, and coordinating law enforcement with behavioral-health services; notes Washington's low officer-to-resident ratio [S2].
