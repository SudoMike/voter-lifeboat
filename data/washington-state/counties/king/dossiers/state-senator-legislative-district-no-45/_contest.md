## What this office does

A Washington state senator is one of 49 members of the State Senate, serving a
four-year term. Senators write and vote on state laws, adopt the state
operating, capital, and transportation budgets, and confirm gubernatorial
appointments. Legislative District 45 covers Eastside communities including
Redmond, Kirkland, Woodinville, Duvall, and Sammamish.
