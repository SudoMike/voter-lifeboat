# State Representative Position No. 2, Legislative District No. 36 — contest overview

- depth: light
- All candidates advance: yes (single candidate; top-two primary sends up to two candidates to the general regardless).

State representatives serve two-year terms; the 36th LD covers northwest Seattle (Ballard, Magnolia, Queen Anne, Greenwood area). Incumbent Liz Berry, House Labor Committee chair and prime sponsor of the 2025 permit-to-purchase firearm law, is the only candidate filed. With no opponent, no issue axes differentiate candidates; the dossier documents her record and platform for voter information only.
