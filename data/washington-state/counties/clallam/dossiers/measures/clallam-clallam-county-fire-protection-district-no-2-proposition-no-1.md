## What it does

Proposition 1 would create a dedicated ten-year property-tax levy for emergency medical services, including basic and advanced life support, beginning with 2027 collections [S1][S2].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: The levy rate would be $0.50 or less per $1,000 of assessed value for ten consecutive years; approval requires the statutory 60% supermajority and turnout threshold [S1][S2].

## Arguments for

The district says a dedicated levy would separate EMS funding from the general fire levy and support staffing as emergency-call demand grows [S2].

## Arguments against

The reviewed official ballot does not include a committee argument against; the direct stated tradeoff is a new dedicated property-tax charge for ten years [S1][S2].

## Axis notes

- New property-tax cost versus dedicated basic and advanced life-support funding and additional staffing [S1][S2].
