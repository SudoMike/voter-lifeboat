## What it does

The measure annexes the City of Tekoa Fire Department into Whitman County Fire Protection District No. 1 if approved in both jurisdictions. [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: City residents would become subject to the district tax levy and eligible to vote in district elections; no model-home estimate is stated. [S1].

## Arguments for

The explanatory statement says annexation supports direct district service, long-term planning, and service delivery. [S1].

## Arguments against

No pro or con volunteers submitted statements after recruitment. [S1].
