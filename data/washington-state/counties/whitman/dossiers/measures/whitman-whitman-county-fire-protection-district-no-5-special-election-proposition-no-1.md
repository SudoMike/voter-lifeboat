## What it does

The measure authorizes a four-year levy for the district general fund's fire-protection maintenance and operations. [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: It raises $10,000 annually from 2027 through 2030 at an approximate $0.21 per $1,000, or about $105 annually on $500,000. [S1].

## Arguments for

The ballot identifies fire-protection maintenance and operations as the use. [S1].

## Arguments against

No official committee arguments were available in the reviewed sample ballot. [S1].
