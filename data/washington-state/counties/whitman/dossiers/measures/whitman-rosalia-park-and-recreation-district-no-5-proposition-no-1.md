## What it does

The measure authorizes two years of taxes for operating, maintaining, and improving the Rosalia Pool. [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: It raises $85,000 in each of 2027 and 2028 at an estimated $0.39 per $1,000, capped at $0.60; about $195 annually on $500,000 at the estimate. [S1].

## Arguments for

The pro statement says stable funding supports maintenance, lifeguards, access, public health, and avoids larger deferred repairs. [S1].

## Arguments against

No against statement was submitted. [S1].
