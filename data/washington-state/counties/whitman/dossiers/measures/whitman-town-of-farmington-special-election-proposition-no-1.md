## What it does

The measure raises $28,000 for street maintenance, repairs and improvements and the town current-expense fund. [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: The estimated levy is $4.18 or less per $1,000 for 2027 collection, or about $2,090 on $500,000 at the estimate. [S1].

## Arguments for

The ballot identifies street work and the current-expense fund as the uses of the revenue. [S1].

## Arguments against

No official committee arguments were available in the reviewed sample ballot. [S1].
