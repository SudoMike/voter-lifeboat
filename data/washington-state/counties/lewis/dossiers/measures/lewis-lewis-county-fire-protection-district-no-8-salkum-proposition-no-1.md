## What it does

Restores the fire and EMS levy to $0.88 per $1,000 and permits nine years of CPI adjustments. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: At the initial stated rate, $88 annually per $100,000 assessed value, or $440 on $500,000; later collections may adjust with CPI. Arithmetic examples are derived from the official rate and are not county model-home estimates [S1].

## Arguments for

The official material identifies the revenue or bond proceeds for fire and EMS operations; no pro committee statement was available in the archived material [S1].

## Arguments against

No con committee statement was available; the direct trade-off is the named fire or emergency-service funding versus higher property taxes and, for the bond, long-term debt service [S1].
