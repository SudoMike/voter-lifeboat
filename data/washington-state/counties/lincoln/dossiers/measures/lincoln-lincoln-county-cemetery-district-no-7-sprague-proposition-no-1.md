## What it does

Proposition No. 1 would authorize Lincoln County Cemetery District No. 7 to collect a one-year excess property-tax levy in 2027 for cemetery maintenance and operation expenses [S1][S2]. The ballot title states that the district board adopted Resolution 2026-01 to place the proposition before voters; a separate copy of that resolution was not included in the reviewed county election materials [S1][S2].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: The district would collect $40,000 in 2027 at an approximate rate of $0.25 per $1,000 of assessed value [S1][S2]. At that stated approximate rate, the levy would equal about $25 for each $100,000 of assessed value, or $125 on a $500,000 property; these are arithmetic illustrations rather than county estimates [S1][S2].

## Explanatory statement

The official local voters' pamphlet contains the ballot title and levy table but no separate explanatory statement [S2].

## Arguments for

No statement in favor was submitted for the official local voters' pamphlet [S2]. The ballot title identifies cemetery maintenance and operation expenses as the levy proceeds' authorized use [S1][S2].

## Arguments against

No statement in opposition was submitted for the official local voters' pamphlet [S2]. The direct ballot tradeoff is the one-year property-tax cost versus $40,000 for cemetery maintenance and operations [S1][S2].
