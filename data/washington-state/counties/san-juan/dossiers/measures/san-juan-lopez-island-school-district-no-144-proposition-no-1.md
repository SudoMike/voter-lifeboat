## What it does

Replaces an expiring levy for educational programs and operations not fully funded by the state for 2027-2030. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Estimated $0.36 per $1,000 each year, or $36 per $100,000 and $180 on $500,000; collections rise from $1,115,000 to $1,235,000. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says the levy supports nursing, library, advanced courses, garden, senior projects, community service, staffing, and classroom resources. [S1]

## Arguments against

The opposition argues against continuing local property-tax burdens; the core tradeoff is school programs against a four-year excess levy. [S1]
