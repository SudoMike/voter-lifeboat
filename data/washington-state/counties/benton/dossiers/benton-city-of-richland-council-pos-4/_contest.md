## What this office does

The council adopts city laws, budgets, taxes, development policy, and service priorities and oversees the city manager [S1].

## Race dynamics

Five candidates contest a three-year unexpired term, contrasting fiscal restraint, growth management, public participation, infrastructure, environmental concerns, and administrative experience [S1].
