## Background

Long reports 28 years as a trial prosecutor handling murder, rape, arson, drug-trafficking, gang, appellate, and state Supreme Court cases [S1][S2].

## Positions

- Supports high bail and trial prosecution for violent defendants and aggressive gang and drug-trafficking enforcement [S1][S2].
- Supports therapeutic and veterans courts for appropriate participants and prioritizing a reported charging backlog [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Long prosecutorial record with enforcement-first positions and limited specialty-court support [S1].
