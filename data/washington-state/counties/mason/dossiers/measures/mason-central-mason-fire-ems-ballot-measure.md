## What it does

Restores the district's regular fire and EMS levy to as much as $1.48 per $1,000 and authorizes a 104% limit factor for three years under Resolution 414. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: At the maximum initial rate, $148 annually per $100,000 assessed value, or $740 on $500,000; later collections may grow under the stated limit factor. Arithmetic examples are derived from the official rate and are not county model-home estimates [S1].

## Arguments for

The official material identifies the revenue for fire protection and EMS operations; no pro committee statement was available in the archived material [S1].

## Arguments against

No con committee statement was available; the direct trade-off is the named public service funding versus the property-tax levy or benefit charge [S1].
