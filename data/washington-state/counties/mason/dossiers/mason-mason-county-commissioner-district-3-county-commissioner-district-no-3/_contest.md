## What this office does

County commissioners adopt the budget, set county policy, oversee departments and public works, and represent the county on regional boards [S1].

## Race dynamics

Seven candidates seek the open District 3 seat: Eric Onisko, Daniel Cameron, Miguel Gutierrez, Rick Lee, Karen Schade Sullivan, George Blush, and Lovejoy Schaefer [S1].
