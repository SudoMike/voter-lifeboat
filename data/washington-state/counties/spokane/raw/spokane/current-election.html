Returning Your Ballot

  • Drop Box: Ballot drop boxes are available starting July 16 and close promptly at 8:00 p.m. on election day, August 4, 2026. View Drop Box Locations
  • Mail: USPS recommends mailing a week before election day. Mailed ballots must be postmarked by Election Day, August 4, 2026.
  • Voter Service Centers:  View Voter Service Center Hours and Locations

Accessible Voting Units

  • An Accessible Voting Unit is available for use at the Spokane County Elections Office Starting July 17 through election day at he Spokane County Elections Office Voter Service Center and at CenterPlace Event Center on election day, August 4th.

Election Results

  • Unofficial Election Results will be available approximately 8:15 PM on August 4th.
  • The Election is certified August 18, 2026.
