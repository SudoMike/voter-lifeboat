## What the office does

The county auditor administers elections and voter registration, records property documents, licenses vehicles and marriages, and performs county financial and payroll functions.

## Race dynamics

This is an open, three-candidate race to succeed retiring Auditor Vicky Dalton. Callie Gee and Dale Whitaker emphasize accounting backgrounds; Michael Cathcart emphasizes elected management, budget oversight, and open-government work.

## Issue axes that genuinely differentiate these candidates

- Professional accounting experience versus elected executive and legislative experience.
- Election access, security, and public confidence.
- Financial transparency, fraud prevention, and modernization of public services.
