## Background

Cathcart has served on the Spokane City Council since 2020 and lists chairmanships involving finance, public safety, regional health, growth management, and public development [S1][S3]. His earlier work includes Better Spokane, the Spokane Home Builders Association, and Washington Senate staff [S1].

## Positions

- Promises scrutiny of county finances, stronger fraud prevention, election integrity, service delivery, and public access to facts [S1][S2].
- Points to open-government work, redistricting reform, attendance, and budget oversight as his relevant record [S1].

## Record

His pamphlet reports two successful City Council elections and service on multiple regional boards, including Spokane Transit Authority [S1].

## Endorsements

His pamphlet lists U.S. Rep. Michael Baumgartner and retiring Auditor Vicky Dalton [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Offers the strongest elected and intergovernmental record in the field.
- Frames the auditor as a watchdog role, while rivals more heavily foreground accounting practice.
