## Background

Gee identifies herself as a public accountant at a CPA firm and says she has managed operations, client service, and finances for her family's business [S1]. She earned a Washington State University business degree with accounting and finance majors [S1][S3]. Her listed elected experience includes precinct committee officer, state Democratic convention delegate, and co-chair of the Spokane County Young Democrats [S1].

## Positions

- Says she would continue the office's election-integrity record through independent oversight, vote-by-mail protection, and broad voter participation [S1][S2].
- Presents financial reporting, compliance, and records management as directly relevant experience and promises transparent, fiscally responsible administration [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Couples election access and vote-by-mail protection with independent oversight.
- Offers private-sector accounting experience but no prior county-office management.
