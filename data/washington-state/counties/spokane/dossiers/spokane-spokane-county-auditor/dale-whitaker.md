## Background

Whitaker is an IRS enrolled agent and owner of a Spokane tax practice; he says he has 18 years of accounting experience [S1][S3]. He was the 2024 Republican nominee for Washington secretary of state and previously worked as a corporate chief financial officer [S1].

## Positions

- Describes the auditor primarily as the county's chief accountant and proposes readable financial statements, modernized services, and close stewardship of public funds [S1][S2].
- Says he would strengthen election security while treating the office as an accounting rather than political position [S1].

## Endorsements

His pamphlet lists Spokane County Treasurer Mike Volz [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Centers technical accounting credentials and fraud detection.
- Election emphasis is security rather than an access-oriented program.
