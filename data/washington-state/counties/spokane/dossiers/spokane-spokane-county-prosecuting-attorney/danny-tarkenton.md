## Background

Tarkenton is a Spokane County deputy prosecutor handling criminal and involuntary-treatment proceedings and reports a docket exceeding 500 cases [S1]. She is an Iraq War veteran with philosophy and psychology degrees, a Gonzaga law degree, and ongoing graduate study in forensic psychology [S1].

## Positions

- Says psychology-informed prosecution can reduce repeat offending and improve public safety [S1][S2].
- Argues prosecutors should serve communities rather than political parties and runs without a party preference [S1].

## Endorsements

Her pamphlet lists Cheney Councilmember Rebecca Long, Spokane Valley Deputy Mayor Tim Hattenburg, Spokane Mayor Lisa Brown, and retired Lt. Col. Molly Marshall [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Greater emphasis on recidivism reduction and forensic psychology.
- Frames political independence as relevant to prosecutorial discretion.
