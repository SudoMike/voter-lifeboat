## Background

McCollam has served as Spokane County prosecutor since July 2025 after more than a decade in criminal and civil prosecution, including chief criminal deputy and interim civil chief roles [S1]. He taught trial advocacy and mock trial at Gonzaga Law [S1].

## Positions

- Promises collaboration with law enforcement, offender accountability, crime-victim advocacy, and what he calls principled, no-nonsense prosecution [S1][S2].
- Opposes policies he says weaken public safety or silence crime victims [S1].

## Record

His pamphlet describes prosecution work in Spokane and Benton counties and training work with narcotics, homicide, and gang-investigator associations [S1].

## Endorsements

His pamphlet lists every Spokane prosecutor since 1969, Sheriff John Nowels, former Rep. Cathy McMorris Rodgers, and Rep. Michael Baumgartner [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Incumbency and senior prosecutor-office management distinguish him.
- Stronger enforcement and institutional-continuity emphasis.
