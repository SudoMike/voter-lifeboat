## What the office does

The elected prosecutor leads felony and juvenile prosecution, represents the county in civil matters, advises county government, and sets office charging and diversion priorities within state law.

## Race dynamics

Both candidates are Spokane County deputy prosecutors and Gonzaga law graduates. Incumbent Preston McCollam emphasizes conventional enforcement, victim advocacy, and office experience; Danny Tarkenton emphasizes reducing repeat offending through psychology-informed approaches and political independence.

## Issue axes that genuinely differentiate these candidates

- Enforcement-first prosecution versus greater emphasis on recidivism reduction and behavioral evidence.
- Institutional continuity versus independence from party and prosecutor-office endorsements.
