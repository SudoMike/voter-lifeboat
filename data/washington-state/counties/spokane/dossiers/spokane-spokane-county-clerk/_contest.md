## What the office does

The clerk maintains Superior Court records, receives filings and fees, supports courtroom administration, and provides public access while protecting confidential information.

## Race dynamics

Dave Lucas emphasizes direct court-administration and military leadership experience. Elliot Robison emphasizes data analysis, user accessibility, workplace relations, and operational reporting.

## Issue axes that genuinely differentiate these candidates

- Direct court-management experience versus technology and workflow expertise.
- Incremental modernization versus expanded guides, notifications, translations, and public metrics.
