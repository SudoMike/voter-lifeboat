## Background

Olson says he served in the U.S. Marine Corps from 1993 to 1997, worked for the Ferry County Sheriff's Office and Republic police from 1998 to 2005, and then served 20 years with the U.S. Border Patrol [S1]. He reports patrol and narcotics-detective assignments in Ferry County and K9 work in human and narcotics detection with the Border Patrol [S1].

## Positions

- Olson says household and property security would be a priority [S1].
- He proposes an aggressive approach to theft and drug abuse [S1].
- He says he would lead the office with transparency, integrity, and compassion [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Olson's stated experience combines local patrol and narcotics work with 20 years in the U.S. Border Patrol [S1].
- His clearest operational priority is stronger enforcement against theft and drug abuse [S1].
