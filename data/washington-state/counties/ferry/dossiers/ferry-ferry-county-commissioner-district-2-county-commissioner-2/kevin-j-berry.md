## Background

Berry says he grew up near Chesaw, Wauconda, Bonaparte, and Curlew; worked summers for the Washington Department of Natural Resources; and served nine years in the U.S. Army [S1]. He reports later managing recreational-vehicle dealerships and working 25 years as a truck driver before returning to Ferry County in 2006 [S1].

## Positions

- Berry proposes supporting local jobs, small businesses, and rural industries [S1].
- He identifies road maintenance and critical infrastructure as priorities [S1].
- He calls for responsible and transparent budgeting and says county policy should protect Ferry County's rural character [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Berry's platform pairs business and job support with roads and infrastructure [S1].
- He describes budgeting in terms of responsibility and transparency but does not offer a specific tax or spending proposal [S1].
