# Ferry County Commissioner, District 2

Washington law assigns county legislative authority to a three-member board of commissioners and empowers the board to manage county property and finances and adopt county ordinances [S1][S2]. The 2026 District 2 primary has three candidates: incumbent Robert Dean, Kevin Berry, and Jeremiah Adams [S3].

The submitted statements differ most on emphasis. Dean stresses fiscal restraint, government efficiency, and continuation of his incumbent record; Berry stresses jobs, small businesses, roads, and infrastructure; Adams proposes using existing county resources to support cottage industry, youth retention, and broader economic opportunity [S3].
