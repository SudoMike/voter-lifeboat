## Background

Dean says he has served as the District 2 Ferry County commissioner for three and a half years [S1]. He reports prior U.S. Army and Air Force service, 20 years as a commercial flight engineer, and membership on regional health, economic-development, EMS, risk, tourism, and recreation bodies [S1]. Official January 2025 Ferry County EMS District No. 1 minutes identify Robert Dean as a board member, corroborating one of those public roles [S2].

## Positions

- Dean emphasizes fiscal responsibility, government efficiency, and transparent management of county funds [S1].
- He says county policy should respect taxpayers while protecting and promoting Ferry County's rural character [S1].
- He seeks continuity in county resource management and government operations [S1].

## Record

- Dean says his commissioner work has focused on contract negotiations, fiscal responsibility, and maintaining county government operations [S1].
- As an EMS District No. 1 board member, Dean signed a January 2025 order transferring $52,000 to the EMS building-construction fund [S2].

## Scoring notes

- Incumbent commissioner experience and membership on several intergovernmental boards distinguish Dean's experience [S1][S2].
- His stated fiscal approach emphasizes efficiency and taxpayer restraint, while the cited EMS record documents approval of a specific interfund transfer [S1][S2].
