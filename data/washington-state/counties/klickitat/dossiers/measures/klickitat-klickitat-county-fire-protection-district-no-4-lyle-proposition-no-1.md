## What it does

Sets the district fire-protection and EMS levy for 2026 and authorizes CPI adjustments for nine years under Resolution 2026-04 [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: $1.15 per $1,000 assessed value, equal to $115 per $100,000 or $575 on $500,000, before later authorized adjustments [S1].

## Arguments for

Approval provides the stated fire and EMS levy revenue; no committee statement was available in the reviewed official material [S1].

## Arguments against

Rejection avoids the proposed rate and adjustment authority; no committee statement was available in the reviewed official material [S1].
