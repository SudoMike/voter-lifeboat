## What this office does

The sheriff leads county patrol, investigations, jail and civil-process functions, staffing, and interagency public-safety coordination.

## Race dynamics

Three law-enforcement candidates seek the office: retired police chief John Bruce and county deputies Tony Warren and Dwayne Matulovich. Their published records distinguish command, local operational, training, and community-service experience.
