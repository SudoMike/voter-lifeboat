## What it does

Proposition 1 would fund expanded health-care services and facilities for Grant County Public Hospital District No. 5, doing business as Mattawa Community Medical Clinic [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: It authorizes a maximum 2027 regular property-tax rate of $0.75 per $1,000 assessed value, annual levy growth of up to 6% through 2035, and use of the 2035 levy as the base for later statutory levy limits [S1].

## Arguments for

The ballot title identifies expanded health-care services and expanded facilities as the limited uses of the revenue [S1].

## Arguments against

No official pro/con committee statements were available in the reviewed sample-ballot source; the principal stated tradeoff is the property-tax increase and multi-year levy growth [S1].
