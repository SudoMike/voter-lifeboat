## What this office does

The assessor annually values taxable real and personal property, maintains property records, and supports equitable distribution of property-tax liabilities.

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Drew Jenkins faces Justin Moriarty in a rematch of the 2025 unexpired-term election. Both have state-recognized appraisal qualifications; Jenkins has the current office and a longer documented appraisal record.
