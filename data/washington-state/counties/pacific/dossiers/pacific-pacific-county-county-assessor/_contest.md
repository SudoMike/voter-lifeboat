## What this office does

The assessor discovers, lists, and values taxable property, maintains property records and maps, and calculates levy rates under state law [S1].

## Race dynamics

The official filing certification lists incumbent Bruce Walker and challenger Jeffrey Phelan [S1]. Approved sources establish a detailed official record for Walker but only filing-level information for Phelan.
