## What this office does

The auditor conducts elections, licenses vehicles and vessels, records documents, issues marriage licenses, and administers county financial functions [S1].

## Race dynamics

The official filing certification lists election administrator Dotsi Graves and incumbent auditor Nicole Deskins [S1]. Both have direct current experience in the office.
