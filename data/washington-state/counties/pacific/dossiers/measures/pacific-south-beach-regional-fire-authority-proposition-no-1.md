## What it does

Authorizes up to $10 million in general-obligation bonds, maturing within 25 years, to replace the regional fire authority's headquarters station [S1].

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Up to $10,000,000 in bonds repaid through annual excess property-tax levies over no more than 25 years; the reviewed official summary does not state a uniform tax rate [S1].

## Arguments for

The official material identifies replacement of the headquarters fire station as the use; no committee argument was available in the reviewed official material [S1].

## Arguments against

No committee argument was available; the direct tradeoff is a replacement public-safety facility versus long-term bond debt and excess property-tax levies [S1].

## Axis notes

- A YES vote authorizes long-term public borrowing repaid by excess property taxes for a fire facility [S1].
