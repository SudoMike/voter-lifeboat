## Background

Sommerfeld has worked as a Pierce County deputy prosecuting attorney since 1995, with both criminal and civil assignments, and has served as a judge pro tem [S2][S3]. He reports advising District Court judges and administrators, Juvenile Court, and the Sheriff's Office on constitutional and procedural matters [S3].

## Positions

- He supports language access, reducing barriers for low-income litigants, therapeutic courts, bias-aware courtroom practice, and representative jury selection [S3].
- He says efficient case resolution must remain consistent with constitutional rights and adequate defense preparation [S3].

## Record

- His three decades in the Prosecuting Attorney's Office include courtroom advocacy and legal advice to county justice institutions [S2][S3].

## Scoring notes

- Broad criminal/civil county-law experience and judge-pro-tem service [S2][S3].
- Explicitly supports therapeutic courts and access-to-justice reforms [S3].
