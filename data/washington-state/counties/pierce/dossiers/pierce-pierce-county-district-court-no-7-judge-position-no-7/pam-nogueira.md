## Background

Nogueira served as an Olympia Municipal Court judge from 2023 to February 2026 and as a Thurston County Superior Court commissioner from 2021 to 2023; she also reports judge-pro-tem assignments in multiple Grays Harbor courts [S2]. Thurston County's official advisory-board roster identifies her as Olympia's municipal judge, and Washington Courts records confirm her District and Municipal Court Judges Association committee work [S3][S4].

## Positions

- Her campaign emphasizes fair treatment, accessible courts, therapeutic-court work, and judicial education [S1][S2].

## Record

- She served on judicial committees concerning civil protection orders, therapeutic courts, court education, pattern jury instructions, diversity, and jail modernization [S2][S3].
- She taught trial advocacy as a University of Washington School of Law adjunct and served on UW Tacoma Legal Pathways' advisory board [S2].

## Endorsements

- Her campaign lists endorsements from sitting and retired judges across Washington; these are self-reported campaign endorsements [S2].

## Scoring notes

- Only candidate in the race with documented full-time judicial and commissioner service [S2][S4].
- Record includes court administration, judicial education, and therapeutic-court work [S2][S3].
