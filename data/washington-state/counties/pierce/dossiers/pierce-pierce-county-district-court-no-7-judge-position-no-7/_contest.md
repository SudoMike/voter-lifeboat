# Pierce County District Court No. 7 — Judge Position No. 7

Pierce County District Court hears misdemeanors, gross misdemeanors, traffic infractions, protection orders, civil matters within its monetary jurisdiction, and other limited-jurisdiction cases [S1]. This open-position race includes Eric J. Lawless, Mike Sommerfeld, and former Olympia Municipal Court Judge Pam Nogueira [S2]. Judicial comparisons should be limited to relevant legal and courtroom experience, court administration, access to justice, and public-safety approaches; partisan inference is inappropriate.
