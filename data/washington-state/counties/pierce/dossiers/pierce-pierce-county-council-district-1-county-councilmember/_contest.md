# Pierce County Council District 1 — County Councilmember

The seven-member Pierce County Council is the county's legislative branch. It adopts ordinances, budgets, and land-use policy and oversees county services. District 1 covers Bonney Lake, Buckley, Carbonado, Crystal Mountain, Graham, Greenwater, Lake Tapps, Orting, South Hill, South Prairie, southeast Auburn, Wilkeson, and nearby unincorporated areas [S1].

This four-candidate race contrasts Jerome O'Leary's business and agricultural background, Terrance Mayers's school-board and public-sector management experience, Kenneth King's behavioral-health and homelessness-services work, and Kelsey Barrans's higher-education and Charter Review Commission experience [S2]. Differentiating issues include growth and infrastructure, public safety, housing and behavioral-health services, transit access, and fiscal oversight [S2].
