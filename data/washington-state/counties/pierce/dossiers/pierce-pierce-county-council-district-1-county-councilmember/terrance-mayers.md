## Background

Mayers is a Bethel School Board director, entrepreneur, and public-sector manager who reports work for Federal Way and King County [S1]. His earlier official statement identifies experience in county emergency management and small-business ownership [S3].

## Positions

- He prioritizes public safety with both community presence and officer accountability [S2].
- He supports road and bridge maintenance, reliable basic services, childcare and school support, workforce development, and assistance for small businesses [S1][S2].
- He says county government should be transparent, accountable, and accessible to residents [S1][S2].

## Record

- He says his school-board work included partnerships for safer sidewalks and opening health clinics in schools [S1].
- He says his public-health work included indoor-air education and outreach in schools, childcare sites, shelters, and faith communities [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Couples public safety with accountability and community presence [S2].
- Frames childcare, schools, and youth services as county investments [S1][S2].
