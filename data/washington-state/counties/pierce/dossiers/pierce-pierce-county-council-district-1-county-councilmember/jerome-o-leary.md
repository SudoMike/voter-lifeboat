## Background

O'Leary describes himself as a senior vice president and commercial-real-estate broker, a partner in a family farm, and a lifelong Pierce County resident [S1]. The County Council confirmed his 2025 appointment to the District 1 seat on the Pierce County Planning Commission, and the county lists him as an elected District 1 Charter Review Commissioner [S2][S3].

## Positions

- He calls for a business-minded approach to housing costs, infrastructure, public safety, homelessness, and long-term stewardship of taxpayer funds [S1].
- He says county government should be efficient and accountable and that decisions should emphasize practical results over partisanship [S1].

## Record

- The County Council unanimously confirmed his Planning Commission appointment in October 2025 [S2].
- He served on the 2026 Charter Review Commission representing District 1 [S3].

## Scoring notes

- Emphasizes fiscal stewardship and government efficiency [S1].
- Links growth policy to real-estate, construction, agriculture, and infrastructure experience [S1].
