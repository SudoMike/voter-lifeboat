## Background

Gese describes herself as a Puyallup Tribal member, commercial geoduck diver, real-estate agent, and operator of a family-owned business [S1].

## Positions

- She emphasizes small-business conditions, government accountability, public safety, infrastructure, and listening across tribal and nontribal communities [S1][S2].
- She calls for growth decisions that respect existing communities and for scrutiny of taxes and county spending [S1][S2].

## Scoring notes

- Differentiates through small-business and tribal background [S1].
- Emphasizes fiscal restraint and accountability [S1][S2].
