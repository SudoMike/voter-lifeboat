## Background

Yambe was appointed to the County Council in 2025 after serving eleven years on the Fife City Council, including as deputy mayor [S2]. His official biography lists regional roles involving transportation, public health, the Tideflats, and tribal relations [S2].

## Positions

- He supports affordable housing, behavioral-health and crisis-response funding, transportation safety, food security, and core public-safety services [S1][S3].
- He argues Pierce County should receive the Sound Transit investments its residents have funded and opposes delaying or reducing South Sound projects [S2].

## Record

- He helped adopt the 2026-27 county budget and identifies funding for district infrastructure, behavioral health, affordable housing, and community organizations as first-year accomplishments [S3].

## Scoring notes

- Incumbent record centers on public investment in housing, health, and infrastructure [S3].
- Supports regional transit investment with an equity rationale [S2].
