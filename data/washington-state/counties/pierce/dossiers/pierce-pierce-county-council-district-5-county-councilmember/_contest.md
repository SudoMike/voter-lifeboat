# Pierce County Council District 5 — County Councilmember

District 5 includes Fife, the Port of Tacoma, Parkland, parts of Spanaway, and portions of east, south, and northeast Tacoma [S1]. Incumbent Bryan Yambe, appointed in 2025 after eleven years on the Fife City Council, faces business owner and Puyallup Tribal member Bettina Gese [S1][S2]. The race differentiates on housing and services, infrastructure and transit, public safety, taxation, and approaches to growth [S2].
