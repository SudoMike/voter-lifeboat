# Pierce County Council District 7 — County Councilmember

District 7 covers Gig Harbor, the Key Peninsula, islands, Ruston, and parts of north and west Tacoma [S1]. The seat is open. Chuck West is a Peninsula School Board member; Brenda Lykins is a former Gig Harbor councilmember and current Charter Review Commissioner; Mike Solan is a Seattle police officer and former police-guild president; Ann E. Jolie filed as a contingency candidate and says she is not actively running [S2][S3]. Key differences concern public safety, taxation and spending, growth, shoreline and infrastructure policy, and health and social services [S2].
