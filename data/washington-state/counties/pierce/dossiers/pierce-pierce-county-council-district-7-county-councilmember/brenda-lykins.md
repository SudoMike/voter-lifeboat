## Background

Lykins is a nurse practitioner who served on the Gig Harbor City Council from 2022 through 2025, including as mayor pro tempore, and served on the 2026 Charter Review Commission [S2][S3].

## Positions

- She campaigns on public safety, responsible growth, infrastructure, affordability, and government responsiveness [S1][S2].
- She links healthcare experience to behavioral health, emergency response, and access to services [S1][S2].

## Record

- Her campaign reports representation of Gig Harbor on the Pierce County Regional Council [S2].

## Endorsements

- Washington Conservation Action endorses Lykins [S4].

## Scoring notes

- Brings municipal-governance and healthcare experience [S2].
- Emphasizes managed growth and environmental stewardship [S1][S4].
