## What it does

Renews a six-year EMS levy at $0.50 per $1,000 of assessed value for the cross-county district. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: At the stated rate, $50 annually per $100,000 assessed value, or $250 on $500,000. Arithmetic examples are derived from the official rate and are not county model-home estimates [S1].

## Arguments for

The official material identifies the revenue or bond proceeds for EMS operations; no pro committee statement was available in the archived material [S1].

## Arguments against

No con committee statement was available; the direct trade-off is the named fire or emergency-service funding versus higher property taxes and, for the bond, long-term debt service [S1].
