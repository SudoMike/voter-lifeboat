## What it does

Authorizes a four-year excess levy raising $200,000 annually for ambulance maintenance and operations, estimated at $0.45 per $1,000. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Approximately $45 annually per $100,000 assessed value, or $225 on $500,000. Arithmetic examples are derived from the official rate and are not county model-home estimates [S1].

## Arguments for

The official material identifies the revenue or bond proceeds for ambulance maintenance and operations; no pro committee statement was available in the archived material [S1].

## Arguments against

No con committee statement was available; the direct trade-off is the named fire or emergency-service funding versus higher property taxes and, for the bond, long-term debt service [S1].
