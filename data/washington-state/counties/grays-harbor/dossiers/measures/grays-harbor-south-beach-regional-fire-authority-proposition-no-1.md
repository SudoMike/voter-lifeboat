## What it does

Authorizes $10 million in general-obligation bonds, repayable over as long as 25 years, to replace the headquarters fire station. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: Up to $10 million principal plus interest, repaid through excess property-tax levies over no more than 25 years; no model-home estimate is stated. Arithmetic examples are derived from the official rate and are not county model-home estimates [S1].

## Arguments for

The official material identifies the revenue or bond proceeds for replacement headquarters construction; no pro committee statement was available in the archived material [S1].

## Arguments against

No con committee statement was available; the direct trade-off is the named fire or emergency-service funding versus higher property taxes and, for the bond, long-term debt service [S1].
