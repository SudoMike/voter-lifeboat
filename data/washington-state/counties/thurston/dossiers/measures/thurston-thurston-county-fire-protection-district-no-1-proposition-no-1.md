## What it does

Authorizes a four-year maintenance and operations levy at about $0.38 per $1,000 to maintain fire and EMS service in Rochester-Grand Mound. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: About $15.83 monthly for a $500,000 home; annual collections rise from $826,166 in 2027 to $956,500 in 2030. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says planned apparatus replacement and facility maintenance preserve readiness and reduce emergency repair costs. [S1]

## Arguments against

No con statement was submitted after recruitment. [S1]
