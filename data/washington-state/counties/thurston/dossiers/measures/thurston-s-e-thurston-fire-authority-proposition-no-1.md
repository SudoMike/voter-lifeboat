## What it does

Authorizes $21.01 million in general-obligation bonds maturing within 25 years to renovate four stations and acquire a tender/pumper, ambulance, and firefighting apparatus. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: The pro committee estimates about $0.20 per $1,000, or $8.33 monthly for a $500,000 home. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says renovations expand staffing capacity and response coverage while adding exhaust-removal and decontamination safety systems. [S1]

## Arguments against

No con statement was submitted after recruitment. [S1]
