## What it does

Authorizes a four-year maintenance and operations levy at about $0.38 per $1,000 to maintain fire and EMS service in Littlerock-Maytown. [S1]

## Fiscal mechanics

Cost line: About $15.83 monthly for a $500,000 home; annual collections rise from $807,020 in 2027 to $934,226 in 2030. [S1]

## Arguments for

The pro committee says predictable apparatus replacement and building maintenance preserve 24/7 readiness and avoid larger failures. [S1]

## Arguments against

No con statement was submitted after recruitment. [S1]
