# Statewide Completeness Note

Election: 2026 Washington primary, August 4, 2026.

The statewide package is treated as complete for the primary election when it
includes every statewide contest that VoteWA marks as appearing on the primary
ballot, plus every statewide measure, if any.

As of 2026-07-17, the statewide primary contests in this package are:

- Justice Position No. 1, Supreme Court
- Justice Position No. 3, Supreme Court
- Justice Position No. 5, Supreme Court
- Justice Position No. 7, Supreme Court

Supreme Court Position No. 4 is intentionally excluded from the August primary
package because VoteWA lists that race's candidates with an election status of
`Advanced to General`, not `In Primary`.

No statewide measures are included for this August primary. The Washington
Secretary of State's current-election materials describe the August measures as
local measures, and the statewide candidate/guide sources did not identify an
August statewide measure.
