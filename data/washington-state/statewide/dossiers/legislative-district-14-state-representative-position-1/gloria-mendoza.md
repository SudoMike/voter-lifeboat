## Background

Mendoza is the incumbent representative and assistant minority whip; she previously served five years as Grandview mayor and three years on its city council [S1][S2].

## Positions

- Emphasizes opposition to income and property-tax increases, farm and small-business protection, public safety, parental rights, religious freedom, and rural jobs [S1][S2].

## Record

- Cites enacted legislation preserving heritage apple orchards and offers state and municipal elected experience [S2].
