## Background

Ley is the incumbent District 18 Position 2 representative and serves on transportation, environment and energy, and postsecondary education and workforce committees [S1].

## Positions

- Supports lower state spending and taxes, property-tax relief, and opposition to a state income tax [S2].
- Supports additional Columbia River bridge corridors, opposes the current replacement approach and Oregon tolling, and emphasizes freight mobility [S2].
- Supports increased law-enforcement resources and a less restrictive policy environment for businesses [S2].
