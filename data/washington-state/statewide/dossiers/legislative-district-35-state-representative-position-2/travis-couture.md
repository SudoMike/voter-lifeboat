## Background

Couture is the incumbent representative, a Navy submarine veteran, former corporate program manager, and former nonprofit operations director [S1][S3].

## Positions

- Opposes Washington's 2026 income-tax legislation and argues for tax and spending restraint [S2].
- Emphasizes government accountability, child-welfare transparency, affordability, public education, and public safety [S2][S3].

## Record

- Serves as House Republican budget leader and on the Economic and Revenue Forecast Council [S2][S3].
