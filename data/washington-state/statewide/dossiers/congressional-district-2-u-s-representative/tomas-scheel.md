## Background

Scheel is an Air Force veteran, software developer, small-business owner, volunteer firefighter, and youth soccer coach [S1]. VoteWA confirms his active District 2 filing and identity [S3].

## Positions

- Supports universal health care, clean-energy transition, major housing investment, reproductive freedom, and access to gender-affirming care [S1][S2].
- Calls for dismantling ICE and replacing it with an immigration system grounded in constitutional rights [S1].
- Pledges to reject corporate PAC and foreign-lobby money and emphasizes accessibility and higher accountability standards for officials [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Combines progressive economic and social policy with public-integrity reform.
- Immigration proposal is more structurally transformative than Larsen's.
