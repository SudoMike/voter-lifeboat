## Background

Feller reports six decades in HVAC work, including service at sensitive U.S. facilities in 60 countries for the State Department [S1]. VoteWA confirms his active District 2 filing and contact identity [S3].

## Positions

- Supports law enforcement, border security, election security, balanced budgets, and Title IX protections for women [S1][S2].
- Attributes affordability problems to high taxes and government growth and emphasizes individual and national sovereignty [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Clearest smaller-government and enforcement-oriented candidate.
- Offers private-sector experience and no elected record.
