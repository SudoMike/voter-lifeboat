## What the office does

A U.S. representative serves a two-year term, writes and votes on federal law and spending, conducts executive oversight, and provides constituent services.

## Race dynamics

Long-serving incumbent Rick Larsen faces three challengers. Edwin Feller runs on smaller government, border security, and law enforcement. Tomas Scheel supports universal health care, clean energy, housing investment, and replacing ICE. Devin Hermanson runs to Larsen's left on wealth, health care, climate, and Israel policy.

## Issue axes that genuinely differentiate these candidates

- Smaller government and lower taxes versus expanded universal programs and public investment.
- Immigration enforcement versus dismantling or constraining ICE.
- Incremental incumbent governance versus structural economic and institutional change.
- U.S. military aid and the Israel-Palestine conflict.
