## Background

Larsen is the incumbent District 2 representative and previously served on the Snohomish County Council [S1][S3]. Congress.gov verifies his congressional identity and legislative record [S4].

## Positions

- Calls health care a right and proposes expanding access and lowering costs [S1][S2].
- Supports increasing affordable-housing supply and infrastructure, job creation, and affordable childcare [S1].
- Supports local law enforcement against fentanyl while requiring ICE agents to follow ordinary law-enforcement rules [S1].
- Opposes tariffs, foreign wars, and executive actions targeting immigrant communities [S1].

## Record

His official congressional page and Congress.gov document his incumbent service and sponsored legislation [S3][S4].

## Scoring notes

- Offers an extensive federal record and incremental policy approach.
- Less structurally transformative on health care and immigration than Scheel or Hermanson.
