## Background

Hermanson says he has raised funds for anti-poverty and women-and-girls programs and launched startups; this is his second run for the office [S1]. VoteWA confirms his active District 2 filing [S3].

## Positions

- Supports taxing billionaires, the Green New Deal, Medicare for All, and rejecting corporate money [S1][S2].
- Calls for ending U.S. aid to Israel and for institutional rebuilding after the Trump administration, including Supreme Court reform [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Most explicit wealth-redistribution and anti-establishment message in the field.
- Foreign-policy position sharply differentiates him from the incumbent.
