## Background

Rule is a third-term state representative and former Blaine councilmember, social worker, mental-health professional, and small-business owner [S1][S2].

## Positions

- Supports small-business tax cuts and opposed tax increases [S1].
- Reports passing rent-increase caps and zoning changes intended to increase housing supply [S1].
- Supports abortion and IVF access, limits on masked ICE agents, data privacy from federal authorities, and replacement funding for Planned Parenthood [S1].
