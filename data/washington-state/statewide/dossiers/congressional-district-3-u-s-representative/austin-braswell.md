## Background

Braswell is a Navy veteran with intelligence-subcontracting, finance, insurance, and signals-analysis experience [S1].

## Positions

- He supports civil liberties, police accountability, keeping ICE out of Vancouver, limits on mass surveillance, due process, and federal transparency [S1][S2].
- He rejects corporate PAC money, supports reducing spending on wars, and calls for protections against AI-related loss of ownership rights [S1].
