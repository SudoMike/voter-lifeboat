## Background

Hennrich worked in theater project management and global Dolby Cinema deployment and serves as a Democratic precinct committee officer [S1].

## Positions

- He supports prescription-drug caps, movement toward universal health care, Social Security, school and infrastructure investment, and relief from student debt [S1][S2].
- He supports replacing the I-5 bridge and investing in rural health, broadband, and flood mitigation [S1].
- He opposes wars and ICE practices, supports higher taxes on the wealthy, and rejects corporate PAC money [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Combines progressive revenue and social investment with reduced war spending [S1].
- Explicitly rejects corporate PAC funding [S1].
