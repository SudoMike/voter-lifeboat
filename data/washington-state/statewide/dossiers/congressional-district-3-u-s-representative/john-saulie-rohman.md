## Background

Saulie-Rohman has 14 years of control-room operations experience in the electric-power industry and no elected experience [S1].

## Positions

- He argues both major parties serve wealthy donors, lobbyists, corporations, and foreign influence rather than voters [S1][S2].
- He criticizes military intervention, erosion of constitutional rights, national debt growth, and concentrated corporate power [S1].

## Scoring notes
