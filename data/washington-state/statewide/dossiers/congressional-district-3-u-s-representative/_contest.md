## What the office does

A U.S. representative writes and votes on federal law and spending, conducts executive oversight, and provides constituent services.

## Race dynamics

Two-term incumbent Marie Gluesenkamp Perez faces eight challengers. State Senate Republican leader John Braun offers the strongest institutional challenge; other candidates span progressive economic and civil-liberties platforms, anti-two-party independents, business-focused centrism, and traditional Republican positions [S1].
