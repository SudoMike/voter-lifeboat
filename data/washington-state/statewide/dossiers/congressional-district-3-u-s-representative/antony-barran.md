## Background

Barran is an entrepreneur, business consultant, and oyster farmer with marketing, hospitality, food, and digital-commerce experience [S1]. He serves on a workforce-development board and reports aquaculture restoration work [S1].

## Positions

- He supports local economies, workforce training, small businesses, affordable energy, practical education, and responsible natural-resource policy [S1][S2].
- He frames his candidacy as nonideological and opposed to political insiders [S1].

## Scoring notes
