## Background

Gluesenkamp Perez has represented District 3 since 2023 and co-owns an auto-repair shop [S1][S2]. She serves on House Appropriations, including its Agriculture and Rural Development subcommittee [S1].

## Positions

- She supports apprenticeships and trades, timber and manufacturing, right-to-repair, cheaper generic drugs, rural health care, infrastructure, and existing social-insurance programs [S1][S3].
- She supports cutting red tape to build housing, abortion rights, vote by mail, and restrictions on corporate consolidation and congressional stock trading [S1].
- She opposes corporate PAC money, tariffs, and presidential wars that raise costs [S1].

## Record

- She introduced legislation to ban congressional stock trading and freeze congressional pay and reports protecting school, road, health, forest, and farmland funding [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Incumbent institutional record paired with anti-corporate and blue-collar framing [S1][S2].
- Housing approach emphasizes supply and red-tape reduction [S1].
