## Background

Roco is a mental-health therapist and former professor who previously sought a U.S. Senate nomination in Hawaii [S1].

## Positions

- He supports removing immigrants he describes as criminals while retaining others, opposes DEI in the military, Portland light rail into Washington, and transgender women competing in women's sports [S1].
- He references a WPA-style housing program and describes mental-health service as central to his work [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Enforcement-oriented immigration and traditional social-policy positions [S1].
