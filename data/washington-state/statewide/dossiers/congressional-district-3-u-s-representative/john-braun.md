## Background

Braun has served in the state Senate since 2013 and is Senate Republican leader; he is also a retired Navy captain and president of a Southwest Washington emergency-vehicle manufacturer [S1][S2][S3].

## Positions

- He supports lower taxes, reduced spending, waste and fraud controls, manufacturing, law enforcement, veterans, Social Security and Medicare, and parental rights [S1].
- He supports border security and national defense [S3].

## Record

- He reports work as a Ways and Means chair and budget negotiator and describes a bipartisan legislative approach [S1][S2].

## Scoring notes

- Longest state elected and military leadership record among challengers [S1][S2].
- Clearest conventional fiscal-conservative platform [S1].
