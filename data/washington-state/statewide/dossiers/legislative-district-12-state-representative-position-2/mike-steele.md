## Background

Steele has represented District 12 since 2017 and is ranking minority member of the House Capital Budget Committee [S1].

## Positions

- Emphasizes capital investment in schools and local infrastructure alongside tax restraint, affordability, public safety, and rural economic development [S1][S2].

## Record

- Offers a long legislative record and senior capital-budget role [S1][S2].
