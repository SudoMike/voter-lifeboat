## Background

Rude has represented District 16 since 2019 and serves as assistant minority floor leader. His prior work includes legislative staff, insurance, and telecommunications [S1][S2].

## Positions and record

- His current committee work emphasizes education, appropriations, and postsecondary education and workforce policy [S2].
- The official pamphlet describes an established legislative record and campaign priorities tied to education and district representation [S1].
