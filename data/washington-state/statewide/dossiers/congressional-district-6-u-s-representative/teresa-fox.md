## Background

Fox reports 30 years in regulatory compliance, safety, risk management, and multimillion-dollar project budgets, including naval and state roles [S1]. She has served five years as a Kitsap County precinct committee officer [S1].

## Positions

- Supports law enforcement and first responders, stronger action against illegal drugs and trafficking, lower taxes, and balanced budgets [S1][S2].
- Prioritizes maritime, timber, naval, and technology jobs and describes environmental policy as practical, science-based stewardship [S1].

## Endorsements

Her pamphlet reports endorsements from county Republican organizations [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Clearest conventional Republican fiscal and public-safety platform.
- Pairs naval expansion and resource industries with environmental stewardship.
