## Background

Randall is the incumbent District 6 representative and previously worked in health care and education access [S1][S3]. She founded the bipartisan Congressional Ferry Caucus [S1].

## Positions

- Prioritizes reducing costs and protecting Medicaid while opposing Trump administration actions on immigration and executive accountability [S1][S2].
- Emphasizes bipartisan district infrastructure, housing, ferry, emergency-management, tribal, and military-family projects [S1].

## Record

Randall reports securing nearly $18 million for projects across the district and using her Oversight Committee role to challenge administration conduct [S1]. Congress.gov documents her legislative activity [S4].

## Endorsements

Her pamphlet lists Sen. Patty Murray, Planned Parenthood Action Fund, NEA, Washington State Labor Council, LCV Action Fund, and Kitsap County Democrats [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Only candidate with a congressional voting and oversight record.
- Combines institutional endorsements and bipartisan local-project claims.
