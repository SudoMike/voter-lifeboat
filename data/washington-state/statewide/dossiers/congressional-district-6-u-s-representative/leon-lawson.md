## Background

Lawson owned an auto dealership on the Squaxin Island Reservation and a record label, and previously ran statewide for governor and U.S. Senate [S1].

## Positions

- Identifies with America First politics and supports secure elections, juvenile rehabilitation tied to workforce development, tribal empowerment, and policies for seniors and veterans [S1][S2].
- Proposes health-care changes aimed at pharmaceutical intermediaries and a blockchain vehicle-title system [S1].
