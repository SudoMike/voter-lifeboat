## What the office does

A U.S. representative serves a two-year term, writes and votes on federal law and spending, conducts oversight, and provides constituent services.

## Race dynamics

First-term incumbent Emily Randall faces four candidates spanning an anti-two-party independent, a public-safety and balanced-budget Republican, a left independent focused on social investment and demilitarization, and an America First Republican.

## Issue axes that genuinely differentiate these candidates

- Federal social investment versus lower taxes and balanced budgets.
- Military and naval investment versus redirecting military spending domestically.
- Immigration enforcement versus abolishing ICE.
- Institutional incumbent governance versus anti-party or populist challenges.
