## What the office does

A U.S. representative serves a two-year term, writes and votes on federal law and spending, conducts oversight, and provides constituent services.

## Race dynamics

Freshman incumbent Michael Baumgartner faces eleven challengers spanning independent economic-populist and anti-party campaigns, several Democratic health-care and affordability platforms, and candidates emphasizing military, diplomatic, legal, medical, or business experience.

## Issue axes that genuinely differentiate these candidates

Health-care system design, taxes and affordability, political money and oversight, immigration, foreign policy, climate and water, AI regulation, and the incumbent-versus-renewal choice distinguish the documented platforms.
