## What the office does

A U.S. representative serves a two-year term, writes and votes on federal law and spending, conducts executive oversight, and provides constituent services.

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Suzan DelBene faces six challengers spanning progressive economic platforms, institutional-reform and independent campaigns, and a Republican smaller-government alternative.
