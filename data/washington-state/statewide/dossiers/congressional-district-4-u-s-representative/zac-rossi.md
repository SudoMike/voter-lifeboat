## Background

Rossi is a Navy submarine veteran and licensed senior reactor operator with engineering-management training and no elected record [S1][S2].

## Positions

- Proposes caps on discretionary-spending growth, reduced farm and student-loan subsidies, later entitlement eligibility, and more private competition in Medicare [S1][S2].
- Also proposes restoring the top tax bracket to 39.6%, capping deductions, and a 1% Medicare payroll-tax increase as part of bipartisan debt reform [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Fiscal-restraint outsider whose plan also includes targeted tax increases and a minimum-benefit protection [S1].
