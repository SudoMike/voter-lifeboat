## Background

Saavedra is the mayor of Toppenish, a councilmember since 2018, a dental hygienist, public-health administrator, minister, and former farmworker [S1].

## Positions

- Supports infrastructure and public-safety funding, affordable health care and housing, clean water, renewable energy, immigration reform, and job growth [S1].
- Emphasizes parental rights, lower taxes, faith and family, and independence from large donors and political establishments [S1][S2].

## Scoring notes

- Local elected record paired with a mixed public-investment, tax-restraint, reform, and traditional-values platform [S1].
