## Background

Boehnke is a state senator and former state representative and Kennewick councilmember, a retired Army lieutenant colonel, and a cybersecurity professor [S1][S2][S3].

## Positions

- Supports dams, hydropower, nuclear energy, energy affordability, agriculture, lower taxes, and less regulation [S1][S2].
- Supports border enforcement, fentanyl interdiction, veterans, seniors, small businesses, faith, and family [S1].

## Record

He cites eight years in the Legislature, including ranking-member work on energy, environment, and technology [S1][S3].

## Scoring notes

- Field's deepest legislative record; enforcement, tax-restraint, and energy-affordability emphasis [S1][S3].
