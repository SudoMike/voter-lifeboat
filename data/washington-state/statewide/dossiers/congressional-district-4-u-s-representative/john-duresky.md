## Background

Duresky is a retired Air Force major and cybersecurity officer who later managed federal technology and contractor-oversight programs at Hanford [S1][S2].

## Positions

- Supports a higher federal minimum wage, unions, infrastructure and broadband investment, and removal of tariffs he considers harmful [S1][S2].
- Supports rural-hospital funding, ACA subsidies, Medicare and Medicaid protection, lower drug costs, and an eventual move toward universal coverage [S1].
- Supports a congressional stock-trading ban, overturning Citizens United, stronger ethics rules, and protection of public lands [S1][S2].

## Scoring notes

- Military and federal-program experience without elected office; reform and current-system-to-universal health agenda [S1].
