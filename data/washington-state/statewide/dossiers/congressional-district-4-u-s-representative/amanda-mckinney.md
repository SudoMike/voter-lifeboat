## Background

McKinney reports six years as a Yakima County commissioner, national county-association leadership, and more than 20 years as a small-business owner and mortgage banker [S1][S2].

## Positions

- Supports agricultural water rights, lower input costs, farm technology, labor reform, and producer-focused trade [S1][S2].
- Supports Columbia and Snake River dams, advanced nuclear and fusion, LNG, border enforcement, law enforcement, and gun rights [S1][S2].
- Emphasizes opposition to government overreach, fraud prevention, and fiscal responsibility [S1].

## Record

She cites county and national advocacy on agriculture, water, wildfire, and rural policy [S1].

## Scoring notes

- Experienced elected candidate with enforcement, energy-affordability, and traditional-values positions [S1][S2].
