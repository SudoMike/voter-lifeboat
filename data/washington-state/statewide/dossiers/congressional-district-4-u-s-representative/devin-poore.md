## Background

Poore is a software engineer with logistics experience and no elected record; he is the Cascade Party nominee [S1][S2].

## Positions

- Supports a stock-trading ban, term limits, campaign-finance reform, and lobbying transparency [S1][S2].
- Supports farm stabilization, workforce programs, decentralized clean energy, wildfire management, and a dignified immigration policy [S1].
- Criticizes costly foreign wars, tax breaks for wealthy people, health-program cuts, and U.S. policy toward Gaza [S1][S2].

## Scoring notes

- New-voice reform candidate with public-investment, immigration, climate, and defense distinctions [S1].
