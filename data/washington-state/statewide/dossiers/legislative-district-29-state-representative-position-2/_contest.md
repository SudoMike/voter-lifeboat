## What this office does

State representatives write and vote on state laws and budgets, conduct oversight, and represent their legislative district [S1].

## Race dynamics

Six candidates seek the open Position 2 seat, including a Tacoma councilmember, housing and youth-service leaders, state-policy professionals, and two Republican challengers. [S1].
