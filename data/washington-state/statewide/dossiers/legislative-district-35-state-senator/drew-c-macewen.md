## Background

MacEwen has served in the Senate since 2023 after a decade in the House and has a Navy and private-business background [S1][S2].

## Positions

- Supports government accountability, restrained and sustainable budgeting, private-sector job growth, education focused on learning, and transportation infrastructure [S2].
- Criticizes the 2026 income tax, recent tax increases, and state spending growth [S3].

## Record

- Serves as Senate Republican deputy leader and ranking member on Environment, Energy and Technology [S2][S3].

## Scoring notes

- Long legislative tenure and explicit fiscal positions are well documented [S1][S2][S3].
