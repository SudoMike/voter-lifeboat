## Background

Berg has served in the House since 2021 after Everett School Board service and has worked as a small-business owner, aerospace professional, and planning commissioner [S1][S2][S3].

## Positions

- Emphasizes public education, small-business and aerospace jobs, housing affordability, public safety, and community investment [S2][S3].

## Record

- Chairs the House Finance Committee and cites work on education funding, tax policy, housing, and economic development [S2][S3].
