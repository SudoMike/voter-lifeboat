## Background

Lekanoff is an eight-year representative with more than 30 years of public-service work spanning health, salmon recovery, climate, infrastructure, housing, workforce, and tribal treaty rights [S1][S2].

## Positions

- Supports balanced budgets, housing, health care, schools, unions and jobs, reproductive freedom, environmental protection, and joint responses to fentanyl and homelessness [S1].

## Record

- Serves on Appropriations, Housing, Health Care and Wellness, and Rules and reports helping fund ferries and accelerate ferry hiring [S1][S2].
