## What this office does

U.S. representatives serve two-year terms, legislate and vote in the House, conduct committee oversight, and provide constituent services [S2].

## Race dynamics

Incumbent Marilyn Strickland faces Democrats Adam Arafat and Alex Scheel, Republican Chris Chung, no-party candidate Derek Maynes, and Union Party candidate Kurtis Engle in the top-two primary [S1]. Arafat and Scheel campaign to Strickland's left on economic or foreign-policy questions; the remaining challengers have sparse verifiable issue material [S1].

## What genuinely differentiates these candidates

- Strickland offers an incumbent record and institutional leadership [S1].
- Arafat emphasizes universal health care, household costs, tax enforcement, and public accountability [S1].
- Scheel emphasizes housing justice and an anti-war foreign policy [S1].
- Evidence is insufficient for comparable claims about Chung, Maynes, or Engle [S1].
